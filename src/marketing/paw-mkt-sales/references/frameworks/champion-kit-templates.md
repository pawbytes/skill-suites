# Champion Kit Templates

Templates for executive summaries, internal FAQs, and champion talk tracks — everything a champion needs to sell internally.

---

### 5.1 Executive Summary Template

*(This is the primary template referenced in SKILL.md Section 7.2)*

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[OUTCOME] for [COMPANY NAME]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Prepared for: [ECONOMIC BUYER NAME, TITLE]
Presented by: [CHAMPION NAME] with support from [YOUR COMPANY]
Date: [DATE]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

[2 sentences written in terms the economic buyer cares about — business risk,
cost, competitive position, or regulatory exposure. Use numbers.]

Example: "Our [TEAM/FUNCTION] currently [DOES THING MANUALLY / USES WORKAROUND]
that costs approximately [HOURS/WEEK × HOURLY RATE] annually and creates
[SPECIFIC RISK — errors / delays / compliance gaps]."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PROPOSED SOLUTION

[PRODUCT NAME] [ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION — what it does and for whom, no jargon].

We evaluated [NUMBER] alternatives including [COMPETITOR/STATUS QUO] and recommend
[PRODUCT NAME] based on [TOP 2-3 EVALUATION CRITERIA].

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

Based on results from [similar customer reference — industry, size]:
• [OUTCOME 1]: [metric] — [timeframe to achieve]
• [OUTCOME 2]: [metric] — [timeframe to achieve]
• [OUTCOME 3 — risk reduction]: [description]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

INVESTMENT AND RETURN

  Annual investment:          $[PRICE] ([TIER NAME])
  Expected year-1 value:      $[ROI] (conservative — based on [ASSUMPTION])
  Net year-1 return:          $[NET = VALUE - PRICE]
  Payback period:             [X] months
  3-year cumulative value:    $[3-YEAR NET]

Key assumption: [STATE THE MOST IMPORTANT ASSUMPTION — e.g., "15% efficiency
gain on reporting tasks for a 5-person team at $85/hr fully loaded"]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

IMPLEMENTATION

  Time to live:               [X] weeks
  Resources from our team:    [X hours from ROLE — or "minimal"]
  Key milestones:             Week 1: [setup] | Week [X]: [training] |
                              Week [X]: full deployment
  Risk mitigation:            [Dedicated onboarding / money-back guarantee /
                              pilot option / rollback plan]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RECOMMENDED NEXT STEP

[CHAMPION NAME] recommends [SPECIFIC NEXT STEP] by [DATE].

[YOUR NAME] from [PRODUCT COMPANY] is available to answer questions directly:
[EMAIL] | [CALENDAR LINK]
```

### 5.2 Internal FAQ Template

```
# Internal FAQ — [PRODUCT NAME] Evaluation

*Prepared by [CHAMPION NAME] for [INTERNAL AUDIENCE]*

---

**Q: Why now — why is this the right time?**
A: [SPECIFIC TRIGGER — regulation deadline / competitive pressure / current
   tool limitation / cost that's only increasing]. Every [TIME PERIOD] we wait,
   [SPECIFIC COST OR CONSEQUENCE].

**Q: Why [PRODUCT NAME] vs. [MOST LIKELY ALTERNATIVE]?**
A: We evaluated both. [COMPETITOR/ALTERNATIVE] is strong on [HONEST STRENGTH],
   but we chose [PRODUCT NAME] because [SPECIFIC REASON TIED TO OUR SITUATION].
   Full comparison available on request.

**Q: What does implementation require from our team?**
A: [HONEST ASSESSMENT — hours, roles, timeline]. The vendor provides
   [DEDICATED RESOURCE / ONBOARDING SUPPORT]. Timeline to fully live: [X weeks].

**Q: What if it doesn't work out — what's the exit?**
A: [CONTRACT TERMS — month-to-month / annual with exit clause / trial period].
   Data export is [AVAILABLE IN FORMAT]. [Any other risk mitigation relevant].

**Q: What does it cost, and where does the budget come from?**
A: [PRICE / TIER]. We plan to fund it from [BUDGET LINE — tools budget /
   ops budget / headcount offset]. The ROI case is outlined in the executive
   summary — it pays for itself in [X months].

**Q: Can we see a reference — someone who's done this?**
A: [YES — [VENDOR NAME] can connect us with [CUSTOMER IN SIMILAR SITUATION] /
   or: "Case study available showing [SIMILAR OUTCOME]"].

**Q: [MOST LIKELY OBJECTION FROM THIS SPECIFIC ORGANIZATION]**
A: [PRE-WRITTEN ANSWER — ask champion what their organization will ask]

**Q: [SECOND MOST LIKELY OBJECTION]**
A: [PRE-WRITTEN ANSWER]
```

### 5.3 Champion Talk Track Template

```
CHAMPION INTERNAL MEETING PREP

Prepared by [YOUR NAME] for [CHAMPION NAME]
For their internal presentation to [APPROVER / COMMITTEE]

---

OPENING (Use your words, not ours):
"I've been evaluating [PRODUCT NAME] for [TIMEFRAME] and I believe it's the
right solution for [SPECIFIC PROBLEM WE DISCUSSED]. Here's why I'm recommending
we move forward."

[Pause. Let them respond before the data.]

THE PROBLEM (in your language):
"Our team currently [SPECIFIC PAIN — use your own description, not vendor language].
This is costing us approximately [YOUR OWN ESTIMATE]. [SHORT STORY OR EXAMPLE
THAT MAKES IT CONCRETE FOR THIS AUDIENCE]."

THE ROI CASE:
"I've modeled the return conservatively. [WALK THROUGH THE ROI SUMMARY FROM
EXECUTIVE ONE-PAGER]. The payback period is [X months]."

ANTICIPATE THESE OBJECTIONS:

If [APPROVER] asks about [COMPETITOR]:
→ "[PRE-SCRIPTED RESPONSE — you and champion agreed on this]"
→ Offer: "I have a comparison document if they want to see it."

If [APPROVER] asks about [IMPLEMENTATION RISK]:
→ "[PRE-SCRIPTED RESPONSE — specific risk mitigation details]"

If [APPROVER] asks about budget:
→ "[WHERE BUDGET COMES FROM + payback period]"

CLOSING ASK:
"I'd like approval to [SPECIFIC NEXT STEP — sign a pilot agreement / start
implementation / move forward with the annual contract]. The timeline is
[DATE] to hit [BUSINESS MILESTONE]."

---

I AM AVAILABLE:
- To join the call if it would help — just add me: [CALENDAR LINK]
- On standby via phone during your meeting: [PHONE]
- To prepare additional materials for any specific concern: [EMAIL]
```
