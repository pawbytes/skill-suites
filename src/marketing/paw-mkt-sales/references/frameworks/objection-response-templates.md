# Objection Response Templates

Structured response cards for the six objection categories using the Acknowledge > Reframe > Respond > Advance framework.

---

### Category 1 — Price and Budget

**Response card format:**

```
OBJECTION: "[EXACT WORDING — e.g., 'It's too expensive']"

REAL CONCERN: [What they're actually worried about — e.g., ROI uncertainty,
               budget reallocation, comparison to alternatives]

ACKNOWLEDGE:
"[Validate the concern without agreeing with the premise.]"
Example: "That's a fair reaction — it's a meaningful investment."

REFRAME:
"[Shift the frame with a question.]"
Example: "Can I ask — what are you paying today for [CURRENT APPROACH],
including the time your team spends on it?"

RESPOND:
"[Deliver the counter with proof, not just assertion.]"
[Insert ROI calculation or customer proof point specific to this objection]

ADVANCE:
"[End with a question that moves forward.]"
Example: "If we could demonstrate ROI in the first 90 days, would the
investment make sense?"
```

Template for each of the 6 objection categories from SKILL.md:

| Category | Key Reframe | Key Proof Required | Advance Question |
|---|---|---|---|
| Too expensive | "What does the current approach actually cost?" | ROI calculation using their numbers | "If we could show [ROI] in 90 days, does it work?" |
| No budget right now | "When does your fiscal year start?" | Pilot at reduced scope / Q+1 proposal | "Could we build the proposal for your [next cycle] review?" |
| Looking for cheaper | "What does 'cheaper' need to accomplish?" | Outcome-per-dollar comparison | "What would make the price feel worth it?" |
| Not the right time | "What would need to change for timing to work?" | Phased implementation that fits their calendar | "What if we started evaluation now, went live after [milestone]?" |
| Evaluating competitor | "What matters most to you in the evaluation?" | Side-by-side on their stated criteria | "Can I put together a comparison on those specific criteria?" |
| Already use competitor | "What made you look at alternatives today?" | Customer who switched from that competitor | "Would it be worth a quick comparison on [the gap they named]?" |
| Need buy-in | "What would make this an easy yes for [approver]?" | Executive one-pager customized to their concerns | "Could we set up 20 minutes with [approver] so I can answer directly?" |
| Happy with current | "What does 'better' look like for you?" | Probe for hidden friction — there's always something | "Would it be worth a 20-minute look, just to confirm you're in the right place?" |
