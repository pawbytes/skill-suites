## By Deal Stage

### Awareness
*(Not typically sales-led — refer to paw-mkt-content and paw-mkt-paid-ads for TOF assets)*

### Discovery
- **Product One-Pager** — leave-behind after first meeting
- **Demo Script** — rep reference during live demo
- **ROI Calculator** — use during meeting to build business case with their numbers

### Evaluation
- **Technical Brief** — share when IT/security team enters evaluation
- **Battle Card** — rep reference when competitor is named
- **[COMPETITOR] Comparison Slide** — share with champion for internal use

### Proposal
- **Executive Summary One-Pager** — for economic buyer who wasn't in earlier meetings
- **Champion Kit** — built per deal for champion to present internally
- **ROI Calculator** — finalized version with agreed assumptions

### Closing
- **Champion Talk Track** — give champion the script for their internal meeting
- **Risk Mitigation Page** — address final objections before signature
- **Reference Customer Names** — for prospect to call / email

### Post-Close
- **Implementation Guide** — handoff to CS team with context from the sale
- **Success Metrics Agreement** — document the outcomes we committed to
