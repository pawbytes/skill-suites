# Email Templates for Sales

Ready-to-customize email templates for post-demo follow-up, proposal cover, re-engagement, and win-back scenarios.

---

### 6.1 Follow-Up After Demo

```
Subject: [COMPANY NAME] — next steps from today

Hi [NAME],

Thanks for the time today. Based on what you shared, I think the biggest
opportunity for your team is [SPECIFIC USE CASE THEY MENTIONED — not generic].

Three things from the demo I want to make sure are clear:

1. [FEATURE tied to their pain #1] — [one sentence recap]
2. [FEATURE tied to their pain #2] — [one sentence recap]
3. [SPECIFIC OUTCOME they asked about] — [one sentence answer]

The most useful next step based on where you are: [ONE CLEAR RECOMMENDATION].

I've attached [RELEVANT ONE-PAGER OR ROI CALCULATOR] — the [ROI calculator
has your team's numbers pre-filled / one-pager is written for [THEIR USE CASE]].

[SPECIFIC QUESTION that moves the deal: "What's the internal process for
decisions like this?" or "Is there a specific date we should be working toward?"]

[YOUR FIRST NAME]
```

---

### 6.2 Proposal Cover Email

```
Subject: [COMPANY NAME] — proposal for [SPECIFIC USE CASE]

Hi [NAME],

Attached is the proposal for [COMPANY NAME].

I've structured it around the three priorities you mentioned:
1. [PRIORITY 1 they named in discovery]
2. [PRIORITY 2]
3. [PRIORITY 3]

The ROI model uses [KEY ASSUMPTION] — let me know if any of those numbers
need adjusting and I'll update the model.

Best path forward from here: [SPECIFIC NEXT STEP — "a 30-minute call with
you and [APPROVER] to walk through the business case" or "your signature on
the pilot agreement"].

Available [SPECIFIC DAYS/TIMES] this week if a call is useful.

[YOUR FIRST NAME]

P.S. The [MOST RELEVANT SECTION] is page [X] — that covers [WHAT THEY CARE
ABOUT MOST].
```

### 6.3 Re-Engagement for Stalled Deals

```
Subject: [COMPANY NAME] — checking in

Hi [NAME],

It's been [TIMEFRAME] since we spoke. I wanted to check in — not to push,
but because [SPECIFIC THING CHANGED that's relevant to them]:

[OPTION A — product news]: We just shipped [FEATURE they asked about or
would care about]. Wanted to make sure you saw it.

[OPTION B — market news]: [RELEVANT MARKET DEVELOPMENT] made me think of
your situation — [ONE SENTENCE connection to their problem].

[OPTION C — honest check-in]: Deals stall for real reasons. Is [ORIGINAL
PROBLEM] still a priority, or has something shifted?

Either way, happy to pick up where we left off or call it — no pressure.

[YOUR FIRST NAME]
```

---

### 6.4 Win-Back for Lost Deals (6+ Months Later)

```
Subject: [COMPANY NAME] — a few things have changed

Hi [NAME],

It's [YOUR FIRST NAME] from [PRODUCT] — we spoke about [6 MONTHS AGO /
LAST YEAR] and ultimately you went a different direction.

A few things worth knowing since then:

1. [RELEVANT PRODUCT UPDATE — the feature or gap that may have influenced
   their decision, if you know what it was]
2. [RELEVANT PROOF POINT — customer result in their industry or use case]
3. [PRICING CHANGE or new tier that may fit their situation better — if applicable]

If your situation is similar to what we discussed, I'd love to reconnect.
If things have changed and [THEIR ORIGINAL PROBLEM] is no longer relevant,
no worries — just wanted to close the loop.

Worth a 20-minute conversation?

[YOUR FIRST NAME]

P.S. We now work with [RECOGNIZABLE COMPANY IN THEIR SPACE / RELEVANT
CUSTOMER TYPE] — happy to share that case study if useful.
```
