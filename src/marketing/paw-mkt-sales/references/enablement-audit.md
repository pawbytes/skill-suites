# Enablement Audit Capability

**Outcome:** Assess what sales collateral exists, what reps actually use, and what gaps need filling before building anything new.

---

## When to Use

Run this audit before creating any sales asset. Wasted collateral is one of the highest-cost problems in B2B go-to-market.

---

## Questions to Ask

Before writing a single slide, gather:

- What collateral does sales currently have? (Deck, one-pager, battle cards, objection guide?)
- Which assets do reps actually use? Which sit untouched?
- What is the typical deal cycle? (Discovery → Demo → Proposal → Close — duration per stage?)
- Who are the buyers? (Economic buyer, technical buyer, champion, end user — who sees each asset?)
- What are the top 3 reasons deals are lost? (Price? Competitor? No decision? Timing?)
- What objections come up in every deal?
- Does the team have a CRM? Can you pull asset usage in won vs lost deals?

---

---

## Golden Rule

"Sales uses what sales trusts." Involve at least one rep in every asset created. Use their customer language, not marketing's internal language. If a rep wouldn't say it in a call, remove it.

---

## Output

Deliver gap analysis with prioritized build list ranked by:
1. Frequency of use (assets reps ask for daily)
2. Deal stage impact (Proposal/Closing stage gaps block revenue)
3. Competitive pressure (battle cards for top 3 competitors mentioned)
