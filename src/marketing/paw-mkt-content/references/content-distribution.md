# Content Distribution Capability

## Overview
Maximize content reach through owned, earned, and paid channels. Apply the 80/20 rule: 20% of time creating content, 80% distributing it.

---

## 1. Owned Channels

| Channel | Action | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Blog | Publish original piece | Day 0 |
| Email newsletter | Feature or excerpt | Within 48 hours |
| Social media | Share natively per platform | Day 0-3 (bridge to paw-mkt-social) |
| Internal | Employee amplification | Day 0 |

---

## 2. Earned Channels

| Channel | Action | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Guest posts | Adapt for external publications | Ongoing |
| Digital PR | Pitch data and insights to journalists | For research/reports |
| Syndication | Republish on Medium/LinkedIn (canonical to original) | Day 7-14 |
| Community seeding | Share in Reddit, Slack, Discord (genuine value, not spam) | Day 1-7 |

---

## 3. Paid Channels

| Channel | Action | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Social ads | Boost top organic performers | After 48-72h organic data |
| Content discovery | Outbrain/Taboola for evergreen content | For high-converting pieces |
| Retargeting | Serve to site visitors and engaged audiences | Ongoing (bridge to paw-mkt-paid-ads) |

---

## 4. Distribution Channels Playbook

| Channel | How to Use | When |
|---|---|---|
| Email newsletter | Send to subscribers, segment by interest | Every piece of content |
| LinkedIn | Publish native post (not just link), share key insights | B2B, thought leadership |
| X/Twitter | Thread summarizing key points, link in final tweet | Tech, media, marketing audiences |
| Reddit | Share in relevant subreddits where you're an active member | When genuinely helpful to the community |
| Facebook Groups | Share in relevant groups with context, not just link drops | Niche communities |
| Hacker News | Submit original research, tools, contrarian takes | Tech, startup audiences |
| Product Hunt | Free tools, calculators, templates | Product launches |
| Syndication | Republish on Medium, LinkedIn Articles (canonical back to original) | Expanding reach |
| Paid promotion | Boost top-performing content on Meta/LinkedIn | High-value pieces, lead magnets |
| Email outreach | Notify people mentioned or quoted in the content | Research, roundups, mentions |
| Internal | Share with sales team, customer success, partners | Every piece of content |

---

## 5. Distribution Time Allocation (Recommended)

| Activity | % of Distribution Time |
|---|---|
| Social media posting and engagement | 25% |
| Email marketing | 20% |
| SEO optimization and internal linking | 20% |
| Community participation (Reddit, forums, Slack) | 15% |
| Outreach (guest posts, link building, PR) | 10% |
| Paid promotion | 10% |

---

## 6. Distribution Checklist

For every piece:
- Published on primary platform
- Shared on all active social channels with native formatting
- Included in next newsletter
- Internal team notified
- Relevant communities identified
- Paid promotion evaluated
- Backlink opportunities identified

---

## 7. Platform-Specific Adaptation

**Adapt format and tone per platform -- never copy-paste.**
- Add new context or angles for each derivative
- Stagger over 2-4 weeks
- Always link back to the original pillar piece
