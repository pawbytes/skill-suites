# Thought Leadership Capability

## Overview
Build authority and influence through original insights, industry analysis, and strategic presence on LinkedIn, guest publications, and original research.

---

## 1. LinkedIn Articles and Posts

- **Articles** (1000-2500 words): Natively published for newsletter distribution and Google indexing. Original frameworks, industry analysis, leadership insights.
- **Posts** (1000-1500 chars): Personal stories with professional lessons. First line is the hook. End with a question. PDF carousel posts drive high engagement.
- **Cadence**: 3-5 posts/week, 1-2 articles/month.

---

## 2. Original Research

Original data generates backlinks, media coverage, shares, and citations.

**Process**:
1. Identify an unanswered question your industry cares about
2. Survey your audience, analyze product data, or compile public data in a novel way
3. Present findings in a branded report with clear methodology
4. Create derivatives: summary blog, infographic, social posts, webinar, podcast episode
5. Pitch to journalists and publications (bridge to PR)

---

## 3. Guest Posts

Publish on industry blogs and partner sites.

- Target sites your audience reads (DA 30+)
- Pitch unique angles, not recycled blog content
- Include a bio linking to a relevant landing page
- Coordinate with paw-mkt-seo for link building value

---

## 4. Speaking and Podcast Opportunities

Thought leadership surfaces speaking and podcast opportunities. When analysis reveals potential:
- Escalate to paw-mkt-pr for speaker placement
- Prepare speaker one-sheet with topics, bio, and past talks
- Pitch to relevant industry podcasts and conferences

---

## 5. Deliverables

**LinkedIn Article** (`thought-leadership/linkedin-article-{slug}-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md`):
- Title optimized for LinkedIn and Google
- Hook for native audience
- Body with original framework or analysis
- CTA to profile or landing page

**Research Report** (`thought-leadership/research-report-{slug}-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md`):
- Executive summary
- Methodology
- Key findings with data
- Implications and recommendations
- Derivative content plan (blog, infographic, social, webinar)
