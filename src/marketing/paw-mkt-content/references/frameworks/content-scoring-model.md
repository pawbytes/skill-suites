# Content Scoring Model

### Quality Rubric (Score 1-10 per dimension)

| Dimension | 1-3 (Poor) | 4-6 (Average) | 7-8 (Good) | 9-10 (Excellent) |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|------------|------------------|
| **Depth** | Surface-level, no new insight | Covers basics adequately | Thorough with useful detail | Comprehensive with expert-level insight |
| **Originality** | Rehashed from other sources | Some unique framing | Original angle or data | First-of-its-kind research or perspective |
| **Accuracy** | Contains errors or outdated info | Mostly correct, basic sources | Well-sourced, verified claims | Expert-reviewed, primary sources cited |
| **Readability** | Disorganized, poor flow | Readable but unremarkable | Well-structured, scannable | Engaging, clear, enjoyable to read |
| **Visual quality** | No images or stock only | Basic custom graphics | Professional design, charts | Interactive, custom illustrations, branded |
| **Actionability** | Theoretical only | Some advice given | Step-by-step guidance | Templates, tools, and executable frameworks |

### Engagement Potential Score
- Search volume of target keyword (0-3 points)
- Social shareability of topic (0-2 points)
- Controversy or novelty factor (0-2 points)
- Visual/interactive potential (0-1 point)
- Timeliness/trending relevance (0-2 points)
- **Total: 0-10 points**

### SEO Value Score
- Keyword difficulty vs. domain authority match (0-3 points)
- Search intent alignment (0-2 points)
- Internal linking opportunity (0-1 point)
- Backlink acquisition potential (0-2 points)
- Content gap (competitors have not covered well) (0-2 points)
- **Total: 0-10 points**

### Conversion Potential Score
- Proximity to purchase decision (0-3 points)
- Natural product/service integration (0-2 points)
- Lead capture opportunity (0-2 points)
- Sales enablement value (0-1 point)
- Retargeting audience value (0-2 points)
- **Total: 0-10 points**

### Composite Content Score
```
Composite Score = (Quality * 0.30) + (Engagement * 0.20) + (SEO * 0.30) + (Conversion * 0.20)
```

| Composite Score | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| 8.0-10.0 | Publish immediately, promote aggressively, invest in repurposing |
| 6.0-7.9 | Publish with standard promotion |
| 4.0-5.9 | Revise before publishing or deprioritize |
| Below 4.0 | Do not publish; rethink the topic or angle |
