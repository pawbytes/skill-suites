# Skyscraper Technique 2.0

### Original Concept (Backlinko, Brian Dean)
1. Find content with lots of backlinks
2. Create something significantly better
3. Reach out to people linking to the inferior piece

### Updated for the AI-Era (2025-2026)

**Step 1: Find link-worthy content**
- Use Ahrefs Content Explorer: filter by referring domains > 50, published in last 2 years
- Search your target keyword and sort by backlinks
- Identify the top 3-5 most-linked pieces

**Step 2: Improve with genuine differentiation**
AI can replicate generic "longer and more detailed" content. You must differentiate with:
- **Original data**: Run surveys, analyze proprietary datasets, scrape public data
- **Expert contributions**: Interview 5-10 industry experts, include named quotes
- **Visual assets**: Custom charts, diagrams, infographics (not stock images)
- **Recency**: Include 2025-2026 statistics, trends, and examples
- **Interactivity**: Calculators, quizzes, self-assessment tools
- **First-hand experience**: Real examples from your own work, not hypothetical

**Step 3: Strategic outreach (updated)**
- **Broken link approach**: Find sites linking to outdated/dead versions of the original content
- **Resource page targeting**: Find curated resource pages in your niche
- **HARO/Connectively/Quoted**: Offer yourself as an expert source referencing your content
- **Digital PR**: Pitch the original data angle to industry publications
- **Social proof first**: Get initial traction via social media, newsletters, and communities before outreaching

### What "Better" Means in 2026
| Dimension | What AI Can Do | What Requires Humans |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------|
| Length and coverage | Easily replicated | -- |
| Data and statistics | Can compile existing data | Original research and surveys |
| Design and visuals | Basic graphics | Custom infographics, branded design |
| Expert quotes | Can fabricate (bad) | Real interviews with named sources |
| Timeliness | Can scrape recent data | Contextualized industry analysis |
| Interactivity | Basic widgets | Custom tools and calculators |
