## Capability Workflow

### 5.1 Google AI Overviews / SGE

To be cited in Google's AI Overviews:
- **Answer directly** in first 1-2 sentences. Front-load, then elaborate.
- **Clear structure**: H2/H3 as questions, bulleted lists, tables for comparisons
- **Cite authoritative sources** within content -- AI favors content referencing data and experts
- **Comprehensive coverage** -- AI pulls from pages demonstrating depth
- **Optimize for PAA** -- answer related questions within same page
- **Structured data** (FAQ, HowTo schema) helps Google parse content for AI features

### 5.2 Optimizing for LLM-Powered Search

ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude optimization:
- **Be the primary source**: original data, unique frameworks, proprietary research. LLMs prioritize original over aggregated.
- **High E-E-A-T signals**: LLMs prefer authoritative, well-cited expert content
- **Clear, unambiguous statements**: LLMs extract concise claims. Avoid hedging on factual expertise.
- **Structured formatting**: definition lists, comparison tables, numbered steps, FAQ blocks
- **Brand mentions across web**: build presence so LLM training data includes brand in relevant contexts
- **Keep content updated**: LLMs with search favor recently updated content

### 5.3 Structured Data for AI Consumption

Beyond standard schema:
- FAQ schema on every relevant page (minimum 3 Q&A pairs)
- HowTo for tutorials
- Speakable for voice search
- About/mentions linking entities to knowledge graph
- Dataset schema for original research

### 5.4 Citation Boost Statistics (Evidence-Based GEO)

| Content Change | Visibility Lift | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| Add source links / cite external authorities | +40% | AI systems prefer content referencing trusted data |
| Add statistics and data points | +37% | Quantified claims are more citable than assertions |
| Add expert quotations | +30% | Third-party attribution signals authority |
| Reduce keyword stuffing | -10% (avoids penalty) | Over-optimization signals low-quality to AI |

**Additional context**:
- Brands cited **6.5x more** via third-party sources than their own domain. Build off-domain presence.
- AI Overviews appear in ~45% of searches (2025-2026). GEO optimization is not optional.

**Practical checklist for GEO-optimized content**:
- [ ] Cites at least 2-3 external authoritative sources with links
- [ ] Includes at least 1-2 specific statistics with sources
- [ ] Includes at least 1 expert quote or third-party attribution
- [ ] Primary keyword density under 2% (no stuffing)
- [ ] Direct answer in first 2 sentences
- [ ] FAQ section with schema

### 5.5 Conversational Content and Source Citation

- "Quick Answer" or "TL;DR" at top of informational content
- Structure: Question > Direct Answer > Detailed Explanation > Evidence
- Natural language headings (real questions, not keyword-stuffed)
- Dedicated FAQ sections on product and service pages
- Clear author attribution with verifiable credentials
- Methodology notes on data content
- Consistent naming, active "About Us" page with external profile links
