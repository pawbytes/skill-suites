# Programmatic & Display Strategy

Guidance for programmatic advertising, DSP selection, CTV, and brand safety. For extended best practices on contextual targeting and Retail Media Networks, see `./references/best-practices.md`.

| DSP | Best For |
|-----|----------|

| Amazon DSP | E-commerce, Amazon shopper data |

---

## 2. Targeting Strategies

### Contextual
- Page content relevance
- Privacy-safe, increasing importance post-cookie

### Behavioral
- Past browsing, purchase intent signals

### First-Party Data
- CRM lists, website visitors, app users
- Most valuable post-cookie

### Geo-Targeting
- DMA, zip code, radius targeting for local campaigns

---

## 3. Connected TV (CTV)

### Creative
- Non-skippable 15s or 30s spots
- Streaming inventory (Hulu, Peacock, Roku, Tubi, Pluto TV)
- Household-level targeting

### Measurement
- Brand lift studies
- Website visit lift
- Complement with retargeting on mobile and desktop

### Creative Specifications
- 16:9 horizontal format, 1920x1080 minimum
- High production value required (big-screen context)
- Audio is critical: CTV is sound-on experience
- Include clear branding throughout
- Add QR codes for direct response campaigns

---

## 4. Brand Safety

### Verification
- Pre-bid brand safety verification (IAS, DoubleVerify, MOAT)
- Inclusion lists over exclusion lists
- Avoid made-for-advertising (MFA) sites

### Monitoring
- Monitor placement reports weekly
- Set viewability thresholds (50%+ in-view for display, 100% in-view for video)

---

## 5. Retail Media Networks (RMN)

### Major Networks (2025-2026)
- Amazon Ads: Sponsored Products, Brands, Display, DSP
- Walmart Connect: on-site search, display, off-site programmatic
- Target Roundel: on-site and off-site
- Kroger Precision Marketing: grocery-specific purchase data
- Instacart Ads: CPG and grocery brands

### Why RMN Matters
- First-party purchase data enables closed-loop attribution
- Advertising at point of purchase captures high-intent shoppers
- Privacy-safe: based on retailer first-party data
