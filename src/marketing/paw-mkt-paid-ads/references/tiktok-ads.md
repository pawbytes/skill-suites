# TikTok Ads Strategy

Guidance for TikTok advertising including Spark Ads and TikTok Shop. For extended best practices on creative and Smart Performance Campaigns, see `./references/best-practices.md`.

---

## 1. Campaign Setup

### Objectives
- Awareness: reach, video views
- Consideration: traffic, app installs, video views
- Conversion: website conversions, catalog sales, lead gen

### Spark Ads
- Boost organic posts from your account or creator accounts
- Higher engagement than standard ads
- Retains social proof (likes, comments, shares)

### Targeting
- Interest and behavior categories
- Custom audiences (pixel, app, engagement)
- Lookalikes, hashtag interaction targeting

---

## 2. Creative Best Practices

### Format Requirements
- Vertical 9:16 only
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Native-feeling content outperforms polished ads

### Hook Strategy
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Text overlays for key messages
- Show the product in use within first 3 seconds
- CTA in final 3 seconds

### Creative Refresh
- Every 7-14 days - creative fatigue hits fast on TikTok
- Rotate 3-5 creatives per ad group minimum

---

## 3. TikTok Shop

### Ad Formats
- Video Shopping Ads: shoppable product cards overlaid on video
- LIVE Shopping Ads: promote live shopping events
- Product Shopping Ads: catalog-based similar to dynamic product ads
- Creator affiliate: partner with creators who earn commission

### Best Practices
- In-app checkout reduces friction dramatically
- Product feed quality matters: clear images, accurate titles, competitive pricing
- Creator content driving to TikTok Shop outperforms brand-produced content
- Combine organic TikTok Shop presence with paid amplification via Spark Ads

---

## 4. TikTok Search Ads

- Keyword-targeted placements in TikTok search results
- Toggle on "Search Ads" within existing in-feed campaigns
- High intent signal: users searching on TikTok have purchase intent
- Creative should answer the search query directly
- Monitor search term reports and add negative keywords

---

## 5. Creator Partnership Framework

1. Brief creators with product samples and 3-5 key talking points (not scripts)
2. Request 3+ content variations per creator
3. Test creator content as Spark Ads with small budgets ($20-50/day per creative)
4. Scale winners to $100-500/day based on CPA performance
5. Negotiate usage rights for 60-90 days minimum
