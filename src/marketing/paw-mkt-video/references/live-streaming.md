# Live Streaming

## Purpose
Plan and execute live streaming strategies -- platform selection, formats, monetization, and live shopping workflows.

## Process

1. **Load Context**: Read brand positioning and audience demographics
2. **Select Platform**: Match platform to audience and objective
3. **Choose Format**: AMA, panel, tutorial, launch, recurring show
4. **Plan Execution**: Pre-stream promotion, during-stream engagement, post-stream repurposing
5. **Deliver Output**: Live stream plan, script/outline, and promotion schedule

---

## 1. Platform Selection

| Platform | Best For | Monetization | Key Feature |
|----------|----------|--------------|-------------|
| YouTube Live | Established channels, long sessions | Super Chat, memberships | Replay as regular video, SEO-indexed |
| Instagram Live | Casual engagement, launches, collabs | Badges, shopping | Up to 3 guests, save to Reels |
| TikTok Live | Young audiences, live shopping | Gifts, TikTok Shop | Real-time product tagging |
| LinkedIn Live | B2B thought leadership, webinars | None native | Professional audience |

---

## 2. Live Shopping

**Pre-stream**:
- Announce 24-48 hours ahead
- Tease products via Stories/posts
- Prepare talking points and pricing

**During-stream**:
- Demonstrate products live
- Answer questions by name
- Offer live-only discounts with time limits
- Pin products

**Post-stream**:
- Clip highlights for Reels/Shorts/TikTok
- Follow up with viewers (bridge to paw-mkt-email)
- Repost replay with product links

---

## 3. Formats

**AMA (Ask Me Anything)**:
- Collect questions beforehand via Stories polls
- Mix pre-submitted and live questions
- 1-2 minutes per answer

**Expert Panel**:
- Co-stream with guests
- Pre-agree on talking points
- Promote across all guests' audiences

**Tutorial / Workshop**:
- Screen share or hands-on demo
- Structured outline
- Encourage real-time follow-along

**Product Launches**:
- Countdown content
- Live reveal and demo
- Launch-day pricing

**Recurring Shows**:
- Weekly or biweekly builds audience habits
- Consistent day, time, and format

---

## 4. Output

Deliver:
- **Live stream plan** with platform, format, duration, and monetization
- **Stream outline/script** with talking points and transitions
- **Promotion schedule** (pre-stream announcements)
- **Post-stream repurposing plan** (clip highlights)
- **Save to**: `./.pawbytes/marketing-suites/brands/{brand-slug}/channels/video/content/scripts/` or campaign path
