# Modern Practices (AI Tools & Emerging Formats)

## Purpose
Apply modern video tools and emerging practices -- AI video generation, vertical video dominance, interactive/shoppable video, and podcast video clips.

---

## 1. AI Video Tools

| Tool | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| Runway | AI video generation, background removal, inpainting, text/image-to-video B-roll |
| Sora | High-quality cinematic text-to-video clips |
| HeyGen | AI avatars, video translation, lip-sync for multilingual scale |
| Synthesia | AI presenter videos from text scripts for training and explainers |
| ElevenLabs | AI voice cloning and TTS for multilingual voice-overs |
| Descript | Text-based editing, auto-transcription, filler word removal |
| CapCut | Mobile editing, templates, auto-captions, native TikTok integration |
| OpusClip | AI long-to-short repurposing -- auto-clip best moments |

**Use AI for**:
- B-roll generation
- Multilingual dubbing
- Prototyping
- Personalization at scale
- Captions
- Repurposing

**Not for**:
- Authentic founder content
- UGC
- Testimonials
- Live streams

**Disclosure**: Disclose AI content per platform policies.

---

## 2. Vertical Video Dominance

9:16 is the default across TikTok, Reels, Shorts, Stories, and increasingly in-feed everywhere.

**Guidelines**:
- Shoot vertical-first for short-form
- For long-form, shoot 16:9 but frame center for vertical cropping
- Every long-form video should produce vertical derivatives

## 3. Interactive and Shoppable Video

**Interactive elements**:
- YouTube cards (polls, quizzes)
- Instagram Story stickers
- TikTok add-ons
- Choose-your-own-path via YouTube cards or platforms (Eko, Wirewax)

**Shoppable features**:
- Tag products in video (TikTok Shop, Instagram Shopping, YouTube Shopping)
- Reduce friction from discovery to purchase
- Live shopping integration

---

## 4. Podcast Video Clips

Record podcasts with video. Extract short clips for maximum repurposing value.

**Process**:
- Record with video (even if audio-only podcast)
- Extract 30-90 second clips of best moments
- Add captions, speaker lower thirds, audiogram waveforms
- One episode produces 5-15 clips

**Best platforms**:
- LinkedIn (top-performing format)
- TikTok
- Reels
