# CTA Frameworks for Video

### The Natural CTA
Integrate the CTA into the content rather than tacking it on.

**Weak**: "Like and subscribe, hit the bell notification."
**Strong**: "If you found this useful, subscribe so you don't miss part 2 where I cover [teaser]."

### CTA Placement
| Placement          | Effectiveness  | Best For                          |
|--------------------|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| Opening (0-10s)    | Low           | Brand mention only; too early for ask |
| Mid-roll (40-60%)  | High          | Subscribe CTA when viewer is engaged |
| End (final 10%)    | Medium        | Follow-up content CTA, link click |
| Pinned comment     | High          | Links, resources, discussion prompt |
| On-screen overlay  | Medium-High   | Throughout; subtle but visible    |
| Description        | Medium        | Links, products, resources        |

### CTA Types for Video
1. **Engagement CTA**: "Comment [word] below if you agree"
2. **Subscribe CTA**: "Subscribe and hit the bell so you don't miss [next video teaser]"
3. **Share CTA**: "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
4. **Link CTA**: "Link in bio / description for [resource]"
5. **Next video CTA**: "Watch this video next for [related topic]" (session time boost)
6. **Save CTA**: "Save this for later when you need it" (algorithm signal)
7. **DM CTA**: "DM me [word] for [free resource]" (Instagram, TikTok)
