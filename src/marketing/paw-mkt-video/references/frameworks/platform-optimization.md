# Platform-Specific Video Optimization

### YouTube Algorithm Optimization
- **Session time is king**: YouTube rewards videos that keep viewers ON YouTube longer
- **CTR x Watch Time**: The core equation; both must be strong
- **Posting schedule**: Consistent schedule (same day/time each week)
- **First 48 hours**: Critical window; maximize initial impressions
- **Playlists**: Group related videos; auto-play increases session time
- **Community tab**: Use for polls, teasers, and engagement between uploads
- **Shorts-to-Long pipeline**: Use Shorts to drive viewers to long-form content

### TikTok FYP Optimization
- **Hook within 1 second**: Faster than any other platform
- **Trending audio**: Use trending sounds for algorithmic boost (even at low volume)
- **Text on screen**: Adds watch time (viewers read + watch)
- **Loop potential**: End the video in a way that flows into the beginning
- **Hashtag strategy**: 3-5 hashtags; mix niche + trending + broad
- **Post time**: Test different times; TikTok is less time-sensitive than others
- **Engagement bait (ethical)**: Ask questions, prompt comments, create debate
- **Series content**: Use TikTok's series feature to encourage follows
- **Reply to comments with video**: Algorithm loves this engagement signal

### Instagram Reels Optimization
- **Cover image**: Design a custom cover image (grid aesthetic matters)
- **Caption matters**: Write a compelling caption (Instagram shows it below Reel)
- **Hashtags**: 5-10 relevant hashtags in caption
- **Share to Stories**: Post your Reel to Stories for additional reach
- **Collab feature**: Use Collaborator tag for cross-audience reach
- **Audio trends**: Use trending audio, but ensure it fits your brand
- **Save-worthy content**: Educational or reference content drives saves (top signal)
- **Cross-promote**: Share Reel in DMs, embed on website

### YouTube Shorts Optimization
- **Vertical format**: 9:16 aspect ratio, under 3 minutes
- **Title optimization**: Shorts titles appear differently; keep under 40 characters
- **Loop structure**: Design for seamless replay (major Shorts signal)
- **Subscribe CTA**: "Subscribe for more" is especially effective on Shorts
- **Hashtag #Shorts**: No longer required but can help categorization
- **Funnel to long-form**: End with "Full video on my channel" or pin comment with link
- **Thumbnail**: Shorts auto-generate thumbnails; design your start frame accordingly
