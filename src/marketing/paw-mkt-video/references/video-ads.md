# Video Ads

## Purpose
Create high-performing video ad scripts and strategies for YouTube pre-roll, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), and TikTok -- including UGC-style formats and direct response formulas.

## Process

1. **Load Context**: Read brand positioning, product, and campaign objective
2. **Select Platform**: YouTube pre-roll, Meta feed/Reels/Stories, or TikTok
3. **Choose Format**: Brand creative, UGC-style, or direct response
4. **Apply Formula**: PAS, BAB, or platform-specific structure
5. **Deliver Output**: Ad script with production direction

---

## 1. YouTube Pre-Roll (Skippable In-Stream)

```
0-5s:   HOOK -- Deliver value or intrigue before the skip button
5-15s:  PROBLEM -- Expand hook, build tension
15-25s: SOLUTION -- Introduce product/brand
25-30s: PROOF -- Testimonial, metric, demo
30s:    CTA -- Click, visit, learn more
```

**Rules**:
- Brand by second 3
- Complete message by second 15 for skip viewers
- 15-60 seconds optimal

---

## 2. Meta Video Ads (Facebook / Instagram)

**Feed ads**:
- Square 1:1 or 4:5
- Hook in 3 seconds
- Captions mandatory
- 15-30s, benefit-led

**Reels ads**:
- 9:16, native-feeling
- 15-30s
- UGC-style outperforms polished

**Stories ads**:
- 9:16
- 5-15s
- Single message, clear CTA

---

## 3. TikTok Ads

- 9:16 only
- 15-30 seconds
- Make TikToks, not ads
- UGC/creator-style outperforms brand creative 2-3x
- Hook in 1-2 seconds
- Captions required
- Creative refresh every 7-14 days

**Spark Ads**: Boost organic creator content -- retains social proof, higher engagement, lower CPA.

---

## 4. UGC-Style Video Ads

Highest-performing ad format across Meta and TikTok in 2025-2026.

```
"I found this [product] and honestly..."     -- Casual testimonial opener
"I was skeptical at first, but..."            -- Objection acknowledgment
"Look at [demonstrates product]..."           -- Product in action
"The thing that surprised me was..."          -- Unexpected benefit
"If you're looking for [solution], try this"  -- Soft CTA
```

**Production**:
- Smartphone, natural lighting, casual setting
- Talking points not scripts
- Multiple takes for authenticity
- Vertical, lo-fi editing

---

## 5. Direct Response Formulas

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)**:
Hook (state the problem) > Agitate (make pain vivid) > Solution (introduce product) > Proof > CTA with urgency

**BAB (Before-After-Bridge)**:
Hook (show "before" struggle) > After (aspirational outcome) > Bridge (product gets you there) > CTA

---

## 6. Ad Metrics Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Excellent | Action if Below |
|--------|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Hook rate (3s views / impressions) | 25%+ | 40%+ | Rework first 3 seconds |
| Hold rate (ThruPlay / 3s views) | 15%+ | 25%+ | Improve pacing |
| CTR (link clicks / impressions) | 1%+ | 2%+ | Strengthen CTA and offer |
| Cost per ThruPlay | Under $0.05 | Under $0.02 | Creative or targeting issue |
| ROAS | 3x+ | 5x+ | Full funnel audit |
