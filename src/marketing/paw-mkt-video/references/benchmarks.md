## YouTube Long-Form Benchmarks

### Click-Through Rate (CTR)
| Context                     | CTR Range     | Notes                              |
|-----------------------------|---------------|-------------------------------------|
| Overall average             | 4-10%         | Impressions to clicks               |
| New channels (<1K subs)     | 3-6%          | Lower baseline, building audience   |
| Established (10K-100K)      | 5-9%          | Sweet spot for growth               |
| Large channels (100K+)      | 4-8%          | Wider audience = more impressions   |
| Exceptional                 | 10-15%        | Viral potential; strong thumb/title |
| Concern threshold           | Below 2%      | Thumbnail or title needs work       |

### Watch Time & Retention
| Metric                        | Benchmark     | Notes                             |
|-------------------------------|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| Average view duration (AVD)   | 50-60%        | Percentage of total video length  |
| First 30 seconds retention    | 70-85%        | Critical hook performance window  |
| Mid-video retention           | 50-65%        | Healthy plateau range             |
| End-screen retention          | 30-50%        | Viewers reaching end screen       |
| Session watch time            | 8-12 min      | Total time viewer stays on YouTube|
| Re-watch rate                 | 5-15%         | Sections replayed (value signal)  |

### Engagement Metrics
| Metric                    | Benchmark Range | Good Performance  |
|---------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Like rate (likes/views)   | 3-5%            | 5%+               |
| Dislike rate              | 0.1-0.5%        | Below 0.5%        |
| Comment rate              | 0.5-1.5%        | 1.5%+             |
| Share rate                | 0.5-1.0%        | 1%+               |
| Subscribe rate (from video)| 1-3%           | 3%+               |
| Save to playlist rate     | 0.5-2.0%        | 2%+               |

### Optimal Video Length (by content type)

## TikTok Benchmarks

### View & Engagement Metrics
| Metric                       | Benchmark Range | Strong Performance |
|------------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| View-through rate (VTR)      | 15-25%          | 30%+              |
| Average watch time           | 50-70%          | 80%+              |
| Loop rate (rewatches)        | 10-25%          | 30%+ (viral signal)|
| Like rate (likes/views)      | 3-8%            | 10%+              |
| Comment rate                 | 0.5-1.5%        | 2%+               |
| Share rate                   | 0.5-2.0%        | 3%+ (strong viral)|
| Save rate                    | 1-3%            | 5%+ (evergreen value)|
| Follow rate (from video)     | 1-3%            | 5%+               |
| Profile visit rate           | 2-5%            | 8%+               |

### Optimal Video Length (TikTok)
| Length                | Performance Notes                                   |
|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| 7-15 seconds         | Easy to loop; good for hook-style content            |
| 15-30 seconds        | Sweet spot for most content types                    |
| 30-60 seconds        | Good for storytelling and educational content        |
| 60-180 seconds       | Growing; favored if watch time is strong             |
| 3-10 minutes         | New extended format; works for deep-dive content     |

### TikTok FYP Ranking Factors (2025-2026)
1. **Watch time / completion rate**: Most important signal
2. **Rewatches (loop rate)**: Strong indicator of quality
3. **Shares**: Weighted heavily for distribution
4. **Comments**: Especially early comments; indicates engagement
5. **Saves**: Signals long-term value
6. **Profile visits**: Interest in the creator
7. **Follows from video**: Conversion signal
8. **Audio usage**: Trending audio gets distribution boost
9. **Posting consistency**: Regular creators get preference
10. **Account interest match**: Content aligned with viewer interests

## Instagram Reels Benchmarks

### Reach & Engagement
| Metric                       | Benchmark Range | Strong Performance |
|------------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Reach rate (vs. followers)   | 20-40%          | 50%+              |
| Non-follower reach %         | 30-60%          | 60%+ (discovery)  |
| Play rate                    | 40-60%          | 70%+              |
| Average watch time           | 50-70%          | 80%+              |
| Like rate (likes/plays)      | 2-5%            | 6%+               |
| Comment rate                 | 0.3-1.0%        | 1.5%+             |
| Share rate                   | 0.5-2.0%        | 3%+               |
| Save rate                    | 1-3%            | 4%+ (best signal) |
| Follow rate (from Reel)      | 0.5-2.0%        | 3%+               |

### Reels Ranking Factors (2025-2026)
1. **Watch time**: How much of the Reel was watched
2. **Saves**: Strongest engagement signal for Reels
3. **Shares** (via DM and Stories): Distribution amplifier
4. **Replay rate**: Indicates compelling content
5. **Comments**: Early engagement matters most
6. **Likes**: Base engagement signal
7. **Audio/trend participation**: Slight boost for trending audio
8. **Account relationship**: Content from followed accounts prioritized in feed
9. **Content freshness**: Recent content gets initial boost

### Optimal Reels Length
| Length           | Performance Notes                                    |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| 7-15 seconds    | Quick hooks, memes, before/after; easy to loop        |
| 15-30 seconds   | Most popular format; tutorials, tips, transitions     |
| 30-60 seconds   | Storytelling, detailed tutorials, product reviews     |
| 60-90 seconds   | Growing; works if retention is strong                 |

## Short-Form Platform Comparison

### YouTube Shorts vs. TikTok vs. Instagram Reels (2025-2026)

| Metric/Feature         | YouTube Shorts        | TikTok               | Instagram Reels       |
|------------------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|
| Max length             | 3 minutes             | 10 minutes            | 90 seconds            |
| Audience size          | 2B+ monthly users     | 1.5B+ monthly users   | 2B+ monthly users     |
| Discovery algorithm    | Shorts shelf + search | FYP (strongest algo)  | Reels tab + Explore   |
| Monetization           | Shorts Fund + Ads     | Creator Fund + LIVE   | Bonuses (limited)     |
| RPM/Revenue            | $0.02-0.10/1K views   | $0.02-0.06/1K views   | Low/variable          |
| Avg. engagement rate   | 3-8%                  | 3-6%                  | 2-5%                  |
| Best for               | Channel growth + SEO  | Virality + trends     | Existing audience     |
| Subscriber conversion  | Good                  | Moderate              | Good (followers)      |
| E-commerce integration | Limited               | TikTok Shop (best)    | Instagram Shopping    |
| Sound-on viewing       | 60-70%                | 90%+                  | 70-80%                |
| Vertical video         | Required (9:16)       | Required (9:16)       | Required (9:16)       |
| Long-form funnel       | Strong (Shorts > Long)| Weak (stays short)    | Moderate              |

### Cross-Posting Strategy
| Approach              | Recommendation                                           |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Same video, all platforms | Acceptable for testing; remove platform watermarks   |
| Adapted per platform  | Best practice; adjust hook, CTA, text overlays, length   |
| Platform-native only  | Ideal but resource-intensive; reserve for top content    |
| Watermark handling    | TikTok watermark on other platforms reduces reach        |

### Equipment Starter Kit Budget
| Setup Level     | Budget        | Includes                                        |
|-----------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Minimal         | $0-200        | Smartphone, natural light, free editing app      |
| Starter         | $200-1,000    | Ring light, lapel mic, basic tripod, CapCut Pro  |
| Intermediate    | $1,000-5,000  | Mirrorless camera, lighting kit, wireless mic, editing software |
| Professional    | $5,000-15,000 | Full camera kit, studio lighting, multi-mic, professional editing suite |

### Cost Efficiency Tips
- **Batch filming**: Film 4-8 videos in one session to reduce setup time
- **Repurpose**: Cut long-form into 5-10 short clips (multiplies content output)
- **Templates**: Use consistent intro/outro, lower thirds, and graphics
- **AI tools**: Use Descript, Opus Clip, Captions.ai to speed up editing
- **UGC**: Supplement produced content with user-generated video (lowest cost, highest authenticity)
