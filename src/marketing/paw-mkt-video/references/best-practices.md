## 1. Short-Form Video (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)

### Platform-Specific Optimization

| Element | TikTok | Instagram Reels | YouTube Shorts |
|---------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| Optimal length | 15-60s | 15-30s | 15-60s |
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 | 9:16 | 9:16 |
| Audio | Trending sounds boost discovery | Trending audio matters | Original audio or trending |
| Captions | On-screen text indexed for search | Text overlays recommended | Auto-captions available |
| Hashtags | 3-5 (mix niche + trending) | 3-5 targeted | 3-5 in description |
| CTA | On-screen text + verbal | On-screen + caption | On-screen + verbal |
| Algorithm signal | Completion rate > shares > comments | Shares > saves > comments | CTR + watch time |

### Hook Frameworks (First 1-3 Seconds)

| Hook Type | Formula | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Question | "Did you know...?" / "What if...?" | "Did you know 90% of startups fail at this?" |
| Bold statement | Counterintuitive claim | "SEO is dead. Here's what replaced it." |
| Pattern interrupt | Unexpected visual or sound | [SLAM desk] "Stop making this mistake." |
| Promise | "I'll show you how to..." | "3 ways to double your email open rate" |
| Myth-busting | "Everyone says X. They're wrong." | "Everyone says post at 9am. Here's why that's wrong." |
| Before/after | Show transformation immediately | [Split screen: before/after] |
| Social proof | "This made our client $100K" | "How we got 10,000 followers in 30 days" |
| Controversy | Polarizing opinion in your niche | "Cold email outperforms every other B2B channel" |
| Tutorial promise | "Here's exactly how to..." | "How to run TikTok ads for $5/day" |
| Curiosity gap | Withhold the payoff | "The one thing every viral video has..." |

### Script Structures

**Problem-Solution-CTA (15-30s):**
1. Hook: State the problem (1-3s)
2. Agitate: Why it matters (3-5s)
3. Solution: Your approach (10-15s)
4. CTA: What to do next (3-5s)

**Tutorial/How-To (30-60s):**
1. Hook: Promise the outcome (1-3s)
2. Steps: 3-5 clear steps with on-screen text (20-40s)
3. Result: Show the outcome (5-10s)
4. CTA: Follow for more / link in bio (3-5s)

**Storytelling (30-60s):**
1. Hook: "I was [relatable situation]..." (1-3s)
2. Conflict: What went wrong (10-15s)
3. Turning point: The discovery/insight (10-15s)
4. Resolution: The result (5-10s)
5. Lesson + CTA (5-10s)

## 2. YouTube (Long-Form)

### YouTube SEO
- **Title**: Keyword in first 3-5 words. Under 60 characters. Curiosity + clarity balance.
- **Thumbnail**: High contrast, emotion (face), minimal text (3-5 words max), bright colors. CTR target: 5-10%.
- **Description**: First 2 lines visible above fold — include keyword and hook. Full description: 300-500 words with timestamps, links, related videos.
- **Chapters**: Use timestamps (0:00 Intro, 2:15 Step 1, etc.) for navigation and SEO.
- **Tags**: 5-10 relevant tags. Primary keyword first. Mix broad and specific.
- **End screens**: Last 20 seconds. Promote next video, subscribe button, playlist.
- **Cards**: 2-3 per video at relevant moments. Link to related content.

### Algorithm Signals
1. **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: Title + thumbnail. Target 5-10%.
2. **Average View Duration**: Most important signal. Higher = better distribution.
3. **Session time**: Does your video lead viewers to watch more YouTube?
4. **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares. Comments weighted heavily.
5. **Upload consistency**: Regular schedule builds algorithmic trust.

### Audience Retention Optimization
- **First 30 seconds**: Must hook and set expectations. Biggest drop-off point.
- **Pattern interrupts**: Every 60-90 seconds, change something (camera angle, graphic, energy).
- **Open loops**: Tease upcoming content to keep viewers watching.
- **Trim ruthlessly**: Cut anything that doesn't add value. Pacing > length.
- **Target**: 50%+ average retention for videos under 10 min. 40%+ for longer.

### YouTube Shorts Strategy
- Shorts have separate algorithm from long-form
- Cross-promote: Shorts can drive subscribers who then watch long-form
- Shorts are now monetizable (revenue share from Shorts Feed ads)
- Repurpose TikTok/Reels content but optimize for YouTube audience

## 3. Live Video

### Live Shopping
| Platform | Format | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| TikTok Live | In-app checkout, product showcasing | Fashion, beauty, food, electronics |
| Instagram Live | Product demos, Q&A, launches | Brand building, DTC |
| YouTube Live | Longer sessions, product reviews, events | Tech, education, gaming |
| Amazon Live | Product demonstrations | Amazon sellers |

- Conversion rates: 3-10x higher than static content
- Schedule lives in advance and promote via Stories/posts
- Engage with comments in real-time (have a moderator if possible)
- Optimal length: 30-60 min for shopping, 60-120 min for events

### Webinar Strategy
- **Registration rate**: Landing page → registered: 30-50%
- **Attendance rate**: Registered → attended: 40-60%
- **Replay rate**: 20-30% watch replay
- **Conversion**: 5-15% of attendees take desired action
- Tools: Zoom, StreamYard, Riverside, Restream (simulcasting)
- Repurpose: Record → edit highlights → YouTube → Shorts/Reels → blog post → email

## 4. Video Production

### Smartphone Production (High Quality, Low Budget)
- **Lighting**: Face a window (natural light) or use a ring light ($30-50). Three-point lighting for polished look.
- **Audio**: Lapel mic ($20-50) or shotgun mic. Audio quality matters more than video quality.
- **Stability**: Tripod ($15-30) or phone mount. Handheld only if intentionally casual/UGC-style.
- **Framing**: Rule of thirds. Eyes at top third line. Clean background.
- **Settings**: 1080p minimum, 4K if storage allows. 30fps standard, 60fps for smooth motion.

## 5. Video Ads

### Creative Best Practices
- **Sound-off design**: 85% watch without sound. Captions and text overlays mandatory.
- **Aspect ratios**: 9:16 for Stories/Reels/TikTok, 1:1 for feed, 16:9 for YouTube/CTV
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds determine if people watch or scroll
- **UGC-style ads**: Outperform polished brand ads 2-4x on Meta and TikTok
- **Length**: 15-30s for conversion, 6-15s for awareness, 60s+ for consideration

### Video Ad Funnel
| Stage | Format | Length | Goal | CTA |
|-------|--------|--------|------|-----|
| Awareness | Broad reach, brand intro | 6-15s | Reach and view | None or subtle |
| Consideration | Testimonial, demo, tutorial | 15-60s | Engagement, traffic | Learn more, watch more |
| Conversion | Product focus, offer, UGC review | 15-30s | Purchase, signup | Shop now, sign up, buy |
| Retargeting | Social proof, urgency, objection handling | 10-20s | Close | Buy now, limited time |
