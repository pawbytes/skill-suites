# See-Think-Do-Care Framework (Google)

## Overview
- Developed by Avinash Kaushik at Google.
- Audience-intent-based framework that maps content and channels to customer mindset.
- Prevents the mistake of only targeting "ready to buy" audiences.

## The Four Audiences

**See (Largest Addressable Qualified Audience)**
- Who: Everyone who *could* buy but is not actively considering.
- Intent: None. They are not looking for your product.
- Content: Brand awareness, entertainment, inspiration, educational content.
- Channels: YouTube, display, social media, content marketing, PR.
- Measurement: Reach, impressions, brand awareness lift, video views.
- Example: A running shoe brand creating content about health and fitness lifestyle.

**Think (Considering, Not Yet Committed)**
- Who: People who have a need and are researching options.
- Intent: Informational. Comparing, evaluating, learning.
- Content: How-to guides, comparison articles, webinars, product demos, reviews.
- Channels: SEO, content marketing, email nurture, retargeting, YouTube.
- Measurement: Engagement, email sign-ups, content downloads, search rankings.
- Example: "Best running shoes for marathon training" comparison guide.

**Do (Ready to Act)**
- Who: People ready to purchase or convert.
- Intent: Transactional. Looking to buy, sign up, or commit.
- Content: Product pages, pricing pages, free trials, demos, promotions, CTAs.
- Channels: Paid search, shopping ads, retargeting, email offers, direct.
- Measurement: Conversion rate, revenue, CPA, ROAS.
- Example: "Buy Nike Pegasus 41 — Free Shipping" landing page with clear CTA.

**Care (Existing Customers)**
- Who: Current customers with 2+ transactions or active subscriptions.
- Intent: Loyalty, support, community.
- Content: Loyalty rewards, exclusive content, product tips, community, support.
- Channels: Email, in-app, community platforms, customer success.
- Measurement: CLV, retention rate, NPS, repeat purchase rate.
- Example: Members-only running community with training plans and gear discounts.

## Implementation Matrix
| Dimension | See | Think | Do | Care |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Budget Share | 15-25% | 25-35% | 30-40% | 10-15% |
| Content Type | Awareness | Educational | Transactional | Retention |
| Primary Metric | Reach | Engagement | Conversion | LTV |
| Typical Channels | Social, Video, PR | SEO, Content, Email | PPC, Retarget, Direct | Email, Community |
| Attribution | View-through | Multi-touch | Last-click | Cohort |
