# RACE Framework (Reach, Act, Convert, Engage)

## Overview
- Created by Smart Insights; used in 100+ countries.
- Customer lifecycle framework covering the full journey from awareness to advocacy.
- Sometimes called PRACE to include the planning stage upfront.
- Complements SOSTAC well: SOSTAC defines the overall plan; RACE structures the tactical execution.

## Stage 1: Plan (The "P" in PRACE)
- Define goals, ICPs (Ideal Customer Profiles), and KPIs before executing.
- Conduct competitor and market research.
- Perform keyword research and audience analysis.
- Establish budget parameters and measurement framework.
- Map existing assets and identify gaps.

## Stage 2: Reach — Build Awareness and Visibility
**Goal**: Drive qualified traffic and grow share of voice.

**Key Tactics**:
- SEO topic clusters and digital PR to grow organic reach.
- Discovery ads, video ads, and social amplification.
- Partnerships, communities, and influencer programs.
- Content marketing for top-of-funnel awareness.

**KPIs**:
- Unique visitors, impressions, reach
- Share of voice (SOV) vs. competitors
- Cost per thousand impressions (CPM)
- Organic search visibility index
- Social media reach and follower growth rate
- Referral traffic volume

**Channel Mix by Stage**:
| Channel | Role in Reach |
|---|---|
| SEO | Long-term organic visibility |
| Paid Social | Targeted awareness campaigns |
| Display / Programmatic | Broad awareness, retargeting seeds |
| Content Marketing | Thought leadership, blog posts, infographics |
| PR / Digital PR | Earned media, backlinks, brand mentions |
| Influencer Marketing | Audience access, social proof |

## Stage 3: Act — Encourage Interaction and Engagement
**Goal**: Drive micro-conversions and engagement that move prospects toward purchase.

**Key Tactics**:
- Lead magnets, webinars, and interactive content (quizzes, calculators).
- Landing pages optimized for conversions.
- Smart retargeting to educational value pages.
- Email capture and nurture sequence entry.
- Social media engagement (comments, DMs, community).

**KPIs**:
- Pages per session, time on site, bounce rate
- Lead magnet download rate
- Email subscription rate
- Blog engagement (scroll depth, comments, shares)
- Social engagement rate (likes, comments, shares / reach)
- Micro-conversion rate (add to cart, create account, start trial)

## Stage 4: Convert — Turn Leads into Customers
**Goal**: Drive macro-conversions (sales, sign-ups, deals closed).

**Key Tactics**:
- Compelling CTAs with clear value propositions.
- Social proof: testimonials, case studies, reviews, guarantees.
- Automated email nurturing and follow-up through CRM.
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO): A/B testing, landing page optimization.
- Sales enablement content (comparison pages, ROI calculators, demos).

**KPIs**:
- Conversion rate (by channel, by campaign)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA) / Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Revenue / transactions
- Average order value (AOV)
- Cart abandonment rate (ecommerce)
- Sales cycle length (B2B)
- Lead-to-customer conversion rate

## Stage 5: Engage — Drive Retention and Advocacy
**Goal**: Increase customer lifetime value and turn customers into advocates.

**Key Tactics**:
- Onboarding email sequences and welcome journeys.
- Loyalty programs and exclusive offers.
- Customer success / support excellence.
- Community building (forums, user groups, social communities).
- Referral programs, UGC campaigns, review solicitation.
- Personalized cross-sell and upsell campaigns.

**KPIs**:
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Repeat purchase rate / retention rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Referral rate and referral revenue
- Customer satisfaction score (CSAT)
- Churn rate
- Social advocacy metrics (UGC volume, brand mentions)

## RACE + SOSTAC Integration
| SOSTAC Phase | RACE Application |
|---|---|
| Situation Analysis | Audit current performance across all RACE stages |
| Objectives | Set SMART goals per RACE stage |
| Strategy | Define audience segments and value propositions for each stage |
| Tactics | Select specific channels and tactics for Reach, Act, Convert, Engage |
| Action | Create implementation plan with deliverables mapped to RACE stages |
| Control | Set up KPI dashboards tracking each RACE stage |
