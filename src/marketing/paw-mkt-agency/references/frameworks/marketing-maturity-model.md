# Marketing Maturity Model

## Overview
- Assessment framework to evaluate how sophisticated a business's marketing operations are.
- Helps agencies set appropriate recommendations based on where the client actually is.
- Derived from CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration) principles adapted for marketing.

## The Five Stages

**Stage 1: Ad Hoc / Reactive**
- Characteristics: No documented strategy, reactive campaigns, inconsistent execution, no analytics.
- Marketing is driven by gut feeling and ad-hoc requests.
- No defined KPIs, no regular reporting, no marketing technology stack.
- Team: Usually 1 person or a business owner doing marketing part-time.
- Agency Recommendation: Start with foundational strategy (SOSTAC), implement basic analytics (GA4), establish brand guidelines, create a content calendar.

**Stage 2: Repeatable / Emerging**
- Characteristics: Some documented processes, basic analytics in place, occasional campaigns.
- Marketing activities are somewhat consistent but not data-driven.
- Basic CRM in place, email marketing started, social media presence established.
- Team: 1-3 people with some marketing skills.
- Agency Recommendation: Formalize strategy, implement marketing automation, establish KPI dashboard, create documented playbooks for recurring activities.

**Stage 3: Defined / Managed**
- Characteristics: Documented strategy, regular campaigns, consistent reporting, defined KPIs.
- Processes standardized across the organization.
- Marketing technology stack in place (CRM, email, analytics, social management).
- Team: Dedicated marketing team (3-8 people) with defined roles.
- Agency Recommendation: Optimize existing channels, implement advanced analytics (attribution modeling, cohort analysis), expand channel mix, A/B testing program.

**Stage 4: Measured / Quantitative**
- Characteristics: Data-driven decisions, multi-channel attribution, continuous optimization, advanced personalization.
- Marketing operations run with clear SLAs and predictable outcomes.
- Advanced martech stack with integration layer (CDP, data warehouse).
- Team: Full marketing department (8-20+) with specialists.
- Agency Recommendation: Implement growth loops, predictive analytics, dynamic personalization, incrementality testing, expand to advanced channels.

**Stage 5: Optimized / Continuous Improvement**
- Characteristics: AI-augmented operations, real-time optimization, test-and-learn culture embedded, marketing as a strategic growth engine.
- Full attribution model, marketing mix modeling (MMM), incrementality testing.
- Highly integrated tech stack with real-time data flows.
- Team: Large, cross-functional marketing organization with center of excellence.
- Agency Recommendation: Serve as strategic advisor, co-innovate on experimental channels, provide specialized expertise for advanced initiatives, act as innovation lab.
