# PESTLE Analysis

**What it is:** Scans the macro-environment across six dimensions: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental.

**SOSTAC Phase:** Situation

**Rapid scan for SMBs — key questions per dimension:**
- **Political:** Any regulatory changes, government policy, or trade conditions that could affect your market?
- **Economic:** How is disposable income trending for your customers? Any inflation, interest rate, or FX exposure?
- **Social:** What demographic or cultural shifts are changing buyer behaviour in your category?
- **Technological:** Which new platforms, tools, or automation are disrupting how you acquire or serve customers?
- **Legal:** GDPR/privacy, advertising regulations, sector-specific compliance — anything active or pending?
- **Environmental:** Sustainability expectations from customers or investors? Supply chain exposure?

**Flag vs. Ignore:** Flag factors that are (a) already affecting revenue or (b) likely to within 18 months. Ignore distant, speculative factors unless the business is in a regulated or resource-dependent sector.
