# Ansoff Matrix

**What it is:** A 2x2 growth strategy matrix across existing/new markets and existing/new products.

**SOSTAC Phase:** Strategy

| | Existing Products | New Products |
|---|---|---|
| **Existing Markets** | Market Penetration (lowest risk) | Product Development |
| **New Markets** | Market Development | Diversification (highest risk) |

**Starting point by brand stage:**
- Early-stage / pre-PMF: Penetration first — prove the core before expanding
- Growth stage: Penetration + Market Development in parallel
- Mature brand: Product Development to avoid commoditisation
- Declining brand: Diversification or exit

**Ansoff → Channel connection:** Market Penetration maps to performance marketing and CRO. Market Development maps to new geo / audience targeting. Product Development maps to content and email nurture for existing lists. Diversification requires entirely new channel strategies.
