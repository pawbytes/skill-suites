# PR Smith's 5S Objectives

**What it is:** Five objective categories — Sell, Serve, Speak, Save, Sizzle — ensuring marketing objectives go beyond just revenue.

**SOSTAC Phase:** Objectives

| S | Objective type | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Sell** | Revenue / leads / conversion | "Generate 200 SQLs/month by Q3" |
| **Serve** | Customer satisfaction / service quality | "Reduce support ticket volume by 20% via self-serve content" |
| **Speak** | Dialogue, community, share of voice | "Grow email list to 10k engaged subscribers by year-end" |
| **Save** | Efficiency, cost reduction, automation | "Automate lead nurture to save 5 hours/week of manual follow-up" |
| **Sizzle** | Brand equity, differentiation, experience | "Achieve a Net Promoter Score of 45+ by Q4" |

**Why it matters:** Plans focused only on Sell objectives neglect the customer experience and long-term brand that make Sell sustainable.
