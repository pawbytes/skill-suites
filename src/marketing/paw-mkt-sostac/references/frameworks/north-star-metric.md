# North Star Metric

**What it is:** A single metric that best captures the core value delivered to customers — if this metric grows, revenue and retention will follow.

**SOSTAC Phase:** Control

**Examples by business model:**
| Model | North Star Metric |
|---|---|
| SaaS / subscription | Weekly Active Users, or feature adoption rate |
| E-commerce | Monthly purchasing customers |
| Marketplace | Transactions per month (both sides active) |
| Media / content | Monthly engaged readers / watch time |
| Professional services | Clients achieving measurable outcome |

**How to select:** Ask "If this number grows consistently, does it guarantee the business is healthier?" If yes, it is a North Star candidate. Must be: (1) measurable, (2) actionable, (3) not a vanity metric, (4) not revenue itself — revenue is the outcome, not the cause.

**North Star ≠ revenue, ≠ page views, ≠ follower count.** These are either outcomes or vanity metrics that can grow while the business declines.
