# Moore's Positioning Statement Formula

**What it is:** A fill-in-the-blank template for writing a precise, differentiated positioning statement.

**SOSTAC Phase:** Strategy

**Formula:**
> "For [target customer], who [has this need or problem], [Brand Name] is the [market category] that [delivers this key benefit]. Unlike [primary alternative], [Brand Name] [key differentiator]."

**Workshopping with a client:** Fill each slot separately. Challenge every slot — "Is that really the primary need?" "What do customers actually call this category?" The differentiation slot must be specific and defensible.

**Weak positioning patterns to avoid:**
- "We are the best quality / most innovative / most customer-focused" — table stakes, not differentiation
- A statement so broad it could apply to three competitors
- Differentiating on a feature customers do not care about
- Using internal language instead of customer language
