# Capability: Objectives Setting

**Outcome:** Define measurable, benchmarked objectives that connect to business goals and cover the full funnel.

**Output:** `./.pawbytes/marketing-suites/brands/{brand-slug}/sostac/02-objectives.md`

**Phase Question:** "Where do we want to be?"

## Frameworks Applied

Load from `./references/frameworks-index.csv` (filter: `sostac_phase=Objectives`):
- OKR Framework
- RACE Framework
- 5S Objectives
- Objective Cascade Method

## Research Work

Using Situation findings:

### 1. Identify Biggest Gaps and Opportunities
Objectives should close critical gaps and exploit strongest opportunities from TOWS analysis.

### 2. Draft Recommended OKRs
Propose primary OKR + 1-2 secondary OKRs with specific Key Results. Base targets on:
- Industry benchmarks
- Brand's current trajectory
- Competitive positioning

**Show your math.**

### 3. Benchmark Every Target
For each Key Result, provide industry benchmark and explain whether target is conservative, realistic, or stretch.

Example: "B2B SaaS median monthly churn is 5-7% (OpenView 2025). Your current 3% is strong — targeting 2.5% is realistic."

### 4. Map to RACE
Show which objectives cover Reach, Act, Convert, Engage. Flag any uncovered stage.

### 5. Build the Objective Cascade
Show how each marketing objective connects upward to business goals and downward to channel-level targets.

## Presentation Format

> "Based on the Situation Analysis, here's what I recommend for your objectives:"

**Recommended Primary OKR** with Key Results, baselines, targets, deadlines — with reasoning for each target

**Benchmark validation table** — your targets vs industry standards

**RACE coverage map** — where objectives focus and any deliberate gaps

**Risks to achievement** — what could prevent hitting these targets

**Key decision points** — 1-2 choices needing user input (e.g., "growth speed vs capital efficiency")

## Validation Questions

- Confirm business-level goals (revenue target, growth expectations)
- Confirm resource constraints affecting achievability
- Let user adjust ambition level on specific KRs

## Success Criteria

- Every KR has baseline, target, deadline, and benchmark comparison
- Objectives address TOWS strategic options from Situation
- RACE coverage is complete or gaps are deliberate and explained
- Cascade shows clear connection from business goals to channel targets
- Risks are identified with mitigation strategies

## SMARTA Validation

Apply to every objective:
- **S**pecific — exact metric and segment defined
- **M**easurable — tied to dashboard metric checked weekly
- **A**chievable — based on benchmarks, not wishful thinking
- **R**elevant — supports business revenue or growth model
- **T**ime-bound — quarterly or monthly deadline
- **A**ligned — connected to company OKRs
