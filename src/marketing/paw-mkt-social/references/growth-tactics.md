# Growth Tactics

> Organic growth strategies, collaboration tactics, viral content frameworks, ethical practices, and social proof loops.
> Load this reference when planning audience growth, viral content, or cross-platform promotion strategies.

---

## 1. Organic Growth by Platform

### Instagram
Reels with trending audio, consistent carousel value, strategic hashtags, Collab posts, engagement in niche communities, cross-promotion from other platforms.

### TikTok
Post frequency (1-3/day during growth phase), trend participation, niche-specific content, consistent posting time, engage in comments on viral posts in your niche.

### LinkedIn
Daily posting, comment strategy on large accounts, employee amplification, LinkedIn newsletters, document posts.

### YouTube
SEO-optimized titles and descriptions, consistent upload schedule, Shorts for subscriber growth, end screen CTAs, community engagement.

---

## 2. Collaboration and Cross-Promotion

- **Collab posts**: Instagram Collab feature shares reach with partner's audience. Choose partners with complementary (not competing) audiences.
- **Cross-platform promotion**: Drive TikTok followers to Instagram, YouTube subscribers to newsletter, etc. Each platform serves a different relationship depth.
- **Brand partnerships**: Joint campaigns, shared content series, co-hosted Lives, giveaways (follow both accounts to enter).

---

## 3. Viral Content Frameworks

No formula guarantees virality, but patterns increase the odds:

- **Emotional trigger**: Content that makes people feel something (surprise, laughter, inspiration, outrage) gets shared.
- **Relatability**: "It's not just me?" moments. Shared experiences within your niche.
- **Novelty**: Show something people have not seen before. Unique angles on familiar topics.
- **Utility**: Extremely practical content people save and share for reference.
- **Debate**: Polarizing (but brand-safe) takes that invite comments. The algorithm rewards comment volume.

---

## 4. Ethical Growth Practices

- Avoid buying followers, likes, or comments -- fake engagement tanks organic reach (platforms penalize it) and erodes credibility when discovered.
- Engagement pods: small groups of creators engaging with each other's content. Can provide an initial boost but should not be the primary strategy.
- Authentic growth takes 6-12 months for meaningful results. Set realistic expectations.
- Focus on engaged followers over follower count. 1,000 engaged followers outperform 100,000 ghosts.

---

## 5. Social Proof Loops

```
Great product/content --> Customer shares UGC --> Brand reposts UGC
--> New customers see social proof --> More purchases --> More UGC
```

Accelerate the loop:
- Make sharing easy (unboxing experience, branded hashtag, share prompts)
- Reward sharers (features, discounts)
- Respond to every piece of UGC

---

## 6. Healthy Follower Growth Benchmarks

| Stage | Monthly Growth | Notes |
|----------------------|----------------|--------------------------------|
| New account (0-1K) | 10-30% | Fastest growth phase |
| Growing (1K-10K) | 5-15% | Content-quality dependent |
| Established (10K-50K) | 3-8% | Consistency matters most |
| Large (50K-500K) | 2-5% | Healthy sustained growth |
| Major (500K+) | 1-3% | Viral moments drive spikes |

### Red Flags
- Sudden spikes without viral content (potential bot followers)
- Follower-to-engagement ratio below 1% (audience quality issue)
- Unfollower rate exceeding 2% per month (content mismatch)
- Engagement dropping while followers grow (shadow ban or bot issue)

---

## 7. Growth Levers by Priority

1. Consistent posting schedule with high-quality content
2. Reels/short-form video (algorithmic boost for reach)
3. Collaborations and cross-promotion
4. Community engagement (comments, DMs, replies)
5. Hashtag and SEO optimization (platform search)
6. Paid promotion of top-performing organic content
7. Cross-platform content repurposing
