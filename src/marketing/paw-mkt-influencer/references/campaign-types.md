# Campaign Types

> Capability prompt for paw-mkt-influencer skill. Load when user needs to select campaign type or structure partnerships.

---

## Sponsored Content

Single or multi-post paid partnerships. Creator produces content featuring the brand.

- **Best for**: Awareness, social proof, traffic
- **Typical deliverables**: 1-3 posts with defined messaging points
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks from brief to publish

---

## Product Reviews and Unboxing

Ship product for honest review. Works best with creators already covering the category.

- **Best for**: High-consideration products, tech, beauty
- **Key principle**: Allow honest feedback -- forced positivity destroys credibility
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks product delivery, 1-2 weeks content creation

---

## Tutorials and How-To

Creator demonstrates product usage.

- **Best for**: Complex products, beauty, tech, SaaS
- **Platform preference**: YouTube tutorials have evergreen SEO value
- **Deliverables**: 5-15 minute demonstration video
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks

---

## Account Takeovers

Creator takes over brand's social account for a day or event.

- **Best for**: Driving creator's audience to brand channel
- **Requirements**: Trust and clear guidelines
- **Timeline**: 1-2 days live, 1 week prep

---

## Co-Created Content

Brand and creator collaborate on content, product collabs, or co-branded series.

- **Best for**: Highest effort, strongest partnership signal
- **Deliverables**: Both parties promote
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks

---

## Affiliate Campaigns

Creator promotes using unique tracking links or codes. Commission-based.

- **Best for**: Performance-driven, conversion focus
- **See full framework**: `./references/affiliate-programs.md`

---

## Brand Ambassador Programs

Long-term partnerships (3-12 months) with consistent creator roster. Ambassadors post regularly, attend events, provide feedback.

### Tiered Program Structure

| Tier | Compensation | Deliverables | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Seed | Free product + commission | 1 post/month | 3-month trial |
| Growth | Monthly stipend + product + commission | 2-3 posts/month | 6-month contract |
| Elite | Retainer + product + commission + event access | 4+ posts/month | 12-month contract |

For detailed tier structure, read `./references/frameworks/ambassador-tiers.md`.

---

## Event Attendance

Invite creators to launches, events, conferences. Cover travel and expenses.

- **Best for**: Product launches, experiential moments
- **Brief**: Key messages but let coverage feel organic
- **Timeline**: Event date + 1 week prep

---

## Long-Term Partnerships

Multi-month contracts with recurring deliverables.

- **Benefits**: Deeper brand integration, audience familiarity, 2-3x better performance than one-offs
- **Compensation**: Monthly retainer or per-deliverable rate
- **Duration**: 3-12 months typical

---

## Decision Framework

Select campaign type based on:

1. **Objective**: Awareness → sponsored/ambassador. Conversion → affiliate/review. Education → tutorial.
2. **Budget**: Tight → gifting/affiliate. Moderate → sponsored/micro. Large → ambassador/macro.
3. **Timeline**: Urgent → sponsored one-off. Ongoing → ambassador/long-term.
4. **Product type**: Simple → unboxing/UGC. Complex → tutorial/review.
