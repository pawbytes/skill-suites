## 1. Cold DM Templates by Platform

### Instagram DM Template

```
Hey {FirstName}! I've been following your content for a while --
your {specific post/Reel description} really resonated with me.

I'm {YourName} from {Brand}. We {one-sentence brand description
with differentiator}.

We're building a creator program around {niche/topic}, and your
style is exactly what we're looking for. Would you be open to
hearing more about a potential collab?

No pressure at all -- just thought there could be a natural fit.

{YourName}
{Brand} | {Title}
```

**DM tips for Instagram:**
- Keep under 150 words. Instagram DMs are read on mobile.
- Reference a specific post or Reel, not just "your content."
- Do not pitch compensation in the first message.
- Include your brand name and title for credibility.
- If they have an email in bio, move to email after initial DM response.

### TikTok DM Template

```
Hey {FirstName}! Just watched your {specific video reference} --
the {specific detail that shows you watched it} was amazing.

I'm with {Brand} and we're looking for creators in the
{niche} space for a paid collaboration.

Interested in hearing more? Can share details here or via email.

{YourName} | {Brand}
```

**DM tips for TikTok:**
- TikTok DMs are even more casual. Keep under 100 words.
- Reference the specific video that caught your attention.
- Mention "paid" upfront -- TikTok creators receive heavy gifting spam.
- Offer to move to email quickly for professional discussion.
- If the creator is on TikTok Creator Marketplace, use that channel first.

### LinkedIn DM Template (B2B)

```
Hi {FirstName},

Your recent post on {specific topic} caught my attention --
particularly {specific insight or data point they shared}.

I'm {YourName}, {Title} at {Brand}. We {one-sentence description
of what the brand does and its differentiator}.

We're exploring partnerships with thought leaders in
{industry/niche} and I believe there's a natural alignment
between your expertise and our audience.

Would you be open to a brief conversation about a potential
collaboration? Happy to share specifics.

Best,
{YourName}
{Title} | {Brand}
{Website}
```

**DM tips for LinkedIn:**
- More formal tone than Instagram/TikTok.
- Reference their specific expertise or thought leadership.
- Mention the value to their audience, not just your brand.
- LinkedIn thought leaders value being recognized for their ideas.
- Follow up with a connection request if you are not already connected.

### Cold Email Template (All Platforms)

```
Subject: Loved your {specific content reference} -- collab with {Brand}?

Hi {FirstName},

I came across your {specific post/video/article} on {platform}
and it immediately stood out -- {1-2 sentences on what you
admired and why it connects to your brand}.

I'm {YourName} from {Brand}. We {one-sentence brand description
and differentiator}. We're building a {campaign type/creator
program} for {niche}, and your work is exactly the kind of
authentic voice we're looking for.

Here's what I had in mind:

- Partnership type: {sponsored content / ambassador / affiliate / etc.}
- Scope: {number of deliverables, platforms, timeline}
- Compensation: {paid collaboration / product + payment / commission}
- Creative freedom: {you bring the concept, we provide guidelines}

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week to discuss?

If email isn't the best way to reach you, happy to DM
on {platform} instead.

Best,
{YourName}
{Title} | {Brand}
{Website} | {Phone optional}
```

**Email tips:**
- Subject line must reference something specific to the creator.
- Keep the email under 200 words for initial outreach.
- Include clear next step (call, reply, DM).
- Mention creative freedom early -- creators value it highly.
- Never send the same template to multiple creators without personalization.

## 2. Follow-Up Sequence Framework

### Standard 4-Touch Follow-Up Sequence

| Touchpoint | Timing | Channel | Content Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Touch 1: Initial Outreach | Day 0 | Email or DM | Value proposition with specific content reference (templates above) |
| Touch 2: Gentle Nudge | Day 3-5 | Same channel | Add new value: reference their recent content, share relevant brand news, or mention what other creators are doing with the brand |
| Touch 3: Channel Switch | Day 7-10 | Alternate channel | Short and casual: "Hey {Name}, dropped you a note on email -- wanted to make sure it didn't get lost. Still interested in chatting about a collab?" |
| Touch 4: Door Open | Day 14-21 | Email | No-pressure close: "Totally understand if the timing isn't right. The door is always open if you'd like to collaborate down the road. Keep up the great work." |

### Follow-Up Message Templates

**Touch 2 (Day 3-5):**
```
Hi {FirstName},

Just following up on my note from {day}. I also wanted to
mention -- your {recent post/video from last few days} was
great. {Brief specific comment}.

We've been working with creators like {similar creator or
general description} on {campaign type} and seeing {brief
result}. Would love to explore something similar with you.

Let me know if you'd like to chat!

{YourName}
```

**Touch 3 (Day 7-10, channel switch):**
```
Hey {FirstName}! Sent you an email a few days ago about a
potential collab with {Brand}. Totally get it if you've been
busy -- just wanted to make sure it didn't slip through.

Happy to chat here or via email ({email}).

{YourName} | {Brand}
```

**Touch 4 (Day 14-21, final):**
```
Hi {FirstName},

I know your inbox is probably flooded, so this will be my
last note. I genuinely admire your content and think there's
a great fit with {Brand}.

If the timing ever feels right for a collaboration, the door
is always open. No pressure at all.

Wishing you continued success!

{YourName}
{Title} | {Brand}
```

### Follow-Up Rules

- Never follow up more than 3 times after the initial outreach (4 touches total).
- Every follow-up must add new value. Never send "just checking in" or "following up."
- If a creator does not respond after 4 touches, add them to a "re-engage in 90 days" list.
- If a creator says no, thank them warmly and mark as "declined -- revisit in 6 months."
- If a creator says "not now," schedule a re-engagement outreach for 60-90 days later.
- Track all outreach in a CRM or spreadsheet: creator name, platform, date, response, status.

## 3. Negotiation Frameworks and Scripts

### The PREP Negotiation Framework

| Step | Action | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **P**repare | Research creator rates, audience data, past partnerships | Check tier benchmarks, engagement rate, content quality |
| **R**ange | Set your budget range (floor, target, ceiling) | Floor: $300, Target: $500, Ceiling: $750 per Reel |
| **E**xchange | Identify value beyond payment to offer | Usage rights, cross-promotion, long-term deal, event access |
| **P**ropose | Lead with mid-range offer and value-adds | "We're thinking $500 per Reel plus product and we'd love to feature your content on our channels" |

### Negotiation Response Scripts

**When a creator's rate is above your budget:**
```
Thanks for sharing your rates, {FirstName}. I really appreciate
the transparency. Your rate of {their rate} is a bit above our
budget for this campaign.

Here's what I can offer:

Option A: {lower rate} per {deliverable} with {value-add:
longer partnership, usage rights included, product, etc.}

Option B: {their rate} per {deliverable} but with a
performance bonus structure: base of {lower rate} plus
{bonus amount} if we hit {metric target}.

Option C: A {number}-month package at {discounted per-post rate}
per deliverable ({total package value}).

Would any of these work for you? Open to discussing what
makes the most sense.
```

**When asked about budget before you've pitched:**
```
Great question! Our budget depends on the scope of the
partnership. To give you an accurate range, it would help
to understand:

- What types of content you'd be most excited to create
- Your typical turnaround time
- Whether you're open to a multi-post or long-term arrangement

Once we align on scope, I can share specific numbers. Our
goal is to create something that feels fair and exciting
for both sides.
```

**When negotiating usage rights:**
```
We'd love to use your content beyond organic posting.
Here's how we typically structure usage rights:

- Organic repost on our channels: Included in base rate
- Paid media license (30 days): +20% of base rate
- Paid media license (90 days): +35% of base rate
- Exclusivity (no competitor posts for {duration}): +50% of base rate

We find most creators prefer the 30-day paid media option
since it keeps overall compensation competitive. What feels
right for you?
```

### Bundling and Discount Strategies

| Bundle Type | Typical Discount | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Multi-post package (3-5 posts) | 10-20% per post | 5 Reels at $450 each vs. $500 each single |
| Long-term contract (3-6 months) | 15-25% per post | 6-month ambassador at $400/Reel vs. $500 one-off |
| Multi-platform package | 10-15% total | Instagram Reel + TikTok video + Stories at 85% of individual rates |
| Usage rights included | 5-10% discount on base | Include 30-day paid media in base rate at slight premium |
| Exclusivity window | No discount; pay premium | Category exclusivity costs 50-100% premium |

## 5. Ambassador Program Tier Structure Template

### Program Overview

```markdown
# {Brand Name} Ambassador Program

## Program Mission
{1-2 sentences on what the program aims to achieve and the
mutual value for brand and ambassadors}

## Program Values
- Authenticity over perfection
- Creative freedom within brand guidelines
- Long-term partnerships over transactions
- Mutual growth and support
```

### Tier Structure

| Attribute | Seed Tier | Growth Tier | Elite Tier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Entry Requirements** | 1K+ followers, brand-aligned content, active engagement | 10K+ followers OR top Seed performer, 3+ months as Seed | 50K+ followers OR top Growth performer, 6+ months as Growth |
| **Contract Duration** | 3-month trial (rolling) | 6-month contract | 12-month contract |
| **Content Requirements** | 1 post/month minimum | 2-3 posts/month minimum | 4+ posts/month minimum |
| **Compensation** | Free product + affiliate commission (10-15%) | $200-$500/month stipend + product + commission (15-20%) | $1,000-$3,000/month retainer + product + commission (20-25%) + event access |
| **Product Access** | Seasonal product shipments | Monthly product + early access to new launches | Unlimited product + exclusive pre-launch access + custom items |
| **Brand Support** | Monthly email newsletter, brand guidelines | Dedicated contact, monthly group call, content feedback | Dedicated account manager, 1:1 strategy calls, priority support |
| **Perks** | Brand feature on social, discount code to share | Co-created content opportunities, website feature | Event attendance, product input, co-design collaborations |
| **Exclusivity** | None required | Soft exclusivity (no direct competitors) | Full category exclusivity |
| **Performance Review** | Monthly (auto-renew if meeting minimums) | Quarterly performance review | Quarterly review with annual renewal negotiation |
| **Promotion Path** | Hit 3-month targets --> Growth invitation | Hit 6-month targets --> Elite invitation | Elite Tier + additional negotiated benefits |

### Ambassador Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring (1-5) |
|---|---|---|
| Content quality and aesthetic | 25% | 1=Poor, 3=Good, 5=Exceptional |
| Engagement rate vs. tier average | 20% | 1=Below 50%, 3=At average, 5=2x+ average |
| Audience alignment with brand target | 20% | 1=No match, 3=Moderate, 5=Perfect match |
| Brand affinity (organic mentions, values) | 15% | 1=None, 3=Some alignment, 5=Genuine fan |
| Posting consistency | 10% | 1=Sporadic, 3=Weekly, 5=Daily |
| Professionalism (past partnerships) | 10% | 1=Issues, 3=Adequate, 5=Outstanding |
| **Minimum score to qualify** | | **3.5/5.0 weighted average** |

## 6. Contract Outline Template

> Note: This is an outline for discussion and planning purposes. Always consult
> legal counsel before using as a binding agreement.

```markdown
# Influencer Partnership Agreement Outline

## 1. Parties
- Brand: {Legal entity name, address}
- Creator: {Legal name, business entity if applicable, address}

## 2. Scope of Work
- Campaign: {campaign name and description}
- Deliverables:
  - {Number} x {content type} on {platform}
  - {Number} x {content type} on {platform}
  - {Any additional deliverables: event attendance, interviews, etc.}
- Timeline: {start date} through {end date}
- Publishing window: Content to be published between {date} and {date}
- Content must remain live for minimum of {90/180/365} days

## 3. Compensation
- Total fee: {amount}
- Payment schedule:
  - {50%} upon contract execution
  - {50%} upon final content approval
  - OR {amount} per deliverable upon approval
- Payment method: {PayPal / wire / ACH / platform}
- Payment terms: Net {15/30} from invoice date
- Performance bonus: {if applicable: bonus amount for exceeding target}

## 4. Content Approval Process
- Creator submits draft content by {date/timeframe}
- Brand provides feedback within {24/48} hours
- Maximum {2} rounds of revisions
- Final approval confirmed in writing (email acceptable)
- If brand does not respond within {48/72} hours, content is deemed approved

## 5. Usage Rights
- Organic posting: {Brand may repost on owned channels with credit}
  - Duration: {campaign duration / 12 months / perpetuity}
- Paid media license: {Brand may use content in paid advertisements}
  - Duration: {30 / 60 / 90 / 180 days}
  - Platforms: {Meta, TikTok, Google, all platforms}
- Whitelisting: {Brand may run ads from creator's account}
  - Duration: {30 / 60 days}
- Editing rights: {Brand may crop, add text overlays, or re-edit}
  - Restrictions: {No alteration of creator's voice or likeness without approval}

## 6. Exclusivity
- Category exclusivity: Creator will not promote {competitor category}
  - Duration: During contract + {30/60/90} days after
- Platform exclusivity: {None / specific platform restrictions}
- Product exclusivity: {None / specific product category}

## 7. Disclosure Requirements
- Creator must comply with FTC Endorsement Guides
- Required disclosures: {#ad / Paid Partnership label / both}
- Brand will provide guidance but creator is responsible for compliance
- Creator must not make claims that are {false, unsubstantiated, medical, etc.}

## 8. Content Ownership
- Creator retains ownership of original content
- Brand receives license as defined in Section 5
- Creator grants brand right to use their name and likeness in
  connection with the campaign

## 9. Confidentiality
- Creator will not disclose campaign details, compensation, or
  strategy to third parties
- Embargo: No public mention before {date}
- Duration: {12/24} months after contract termination

## 10. Termination
- Either party may terminate with {14/30} days written notice
- Immediate termination for: material breach, illegal activity,
  conduct that damages brand reputation
- Upon termination: completed deliverables are still compensated;
  remaining deliverables are canceled
- Usage rights for completed content survive termination per Section 5

## 11. Performance Benchmarks (if applicable)
- Minimum engagement rate: {X%}
- Minimum views: {X} per deliverable
- If benchmarks are not met for {2/3} consecutive deliverables,
  brand may renegotiate terms or terminate

## 12. Representations and Warranties
- Creator represents they have authority to enter this agreement
- Creator represents their audience is genuine and not artificially inflated
- Creator represents they will create original content
- Creator will not use AI to generate content without brand approval
```

## 8. Influencer Scoring Rubric

### Scoring Matrix (Use During Vetting)

| Criterion | Weight | 1 (Poor) | 2 (Below Avg) | 3 (Average) | 4 (Good) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Engagement Rate** | 20% | Below 50% of tier avg | 50-75% of tier avg | At tier average | 1.5x tier avg | 2x+ tier avg |
| **Audience Quality** | 20% | Major mismatch with target | Some overlap | Moderate alignment | Strong alignment | Perfect match (geo, demo, interests) |
| **Content Quality** | 15% | Low effort, inconsistent | Acceptable but generic | Good production, authentic voice | High quality, distinctive style | Exceptional creative, trendsetting |
| **Brand Fit** | 15% | Conflicting values or category | Neutral, no natural connection | General category relevance | Strong category fit, value alignment | Organic fan, natural advocate |
| **Authenticity** | 15% | Red flags (fake followers, bots) | Some questionable signals | Appears genuine, limited data | Strong signals of genuine audience | Verified authentic, community-driven |
| **Posting Consistency** | 5% | Inactive or sporadic | Monthly at best | 2-3x per week | Daily or near-daily | Consistent schedule, high volume |
| **Past Partnerships** | 5% | Compliance issues, poor execution | Limited experience | Adequate, standard executions | Professional, clear disclosures | Outstanding track record, case studies |
| **Professionalism** | 5% | Unresponsive, unreliable | Slow responses, some issues | Responsive, meets deadlines | Proactive communication, on time | Exceeds expectations, adds value |

### Scoring Thresholds

| Score Range | Recommendation | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 4.5-5.0 | Tier 1: Priority partner | Fast-track outreach, offer premium terms |
| 3.5-4.4 | Tier 2: Strong candidate | Standard outreach, competitive offer |
| 2.5-3.4 | Tier 3: Consider for volume | Seed tier or gifting-only partnership |
| Below 2.5 | Pass | Do not pursue; revisit in 6 months |

### Quick-Score Checklist (Rapid Vetting)

Use this 5-question checklist for initial screening before full scoring:

- [ ] Engagement rate at or above tier average?
- [ ] Audience demographics match brand target? (check top countries, age range)
- [ ] Content aesthetic and values align with brand?
- [ ] No red flags? (fake followers, missing disclosures, competitor partnerships)
- [ ] Active and consistent posting in the last 30 days?

If all 5 boxes are checked, proceed to full scoring. If 2+ boxes fail, pass.

|---|---|---|
