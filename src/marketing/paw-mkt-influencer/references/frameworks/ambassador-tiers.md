# Ambassador Program Tier Structure Template

### Program Overview

```markdown
# {Brand Name} Ambassador Program

## Program Mission
{1-2 sentences on what the program aims to achieve and the
mutual value for brand and ambassadors}

## Program Values
- Authenticity over perfection
- Creative freedom within brand guidelines
- Long-term partnerships over transactions
- Mutual growth and support
```

### Tier Structure

| Attribute | Seed Tier | Growth Tier | Elite Tier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Entry Requirements** | 1K+ followers, brand-aligned content, active engagement | 10K+ followers OR top Seed performer, 3+ months as Seed | 50K+ followers OR top Growth performer, 6+ months as Growth |
| **Contract Duration** | 3-month trial (rolling) | 6-month contract | 12-month contract |
| **Content Requirements** | 1 post/month minimum | 2-3 posts/month minimum | 4+ posts/month minimum |
| **Compensation** | Free product + affiliate commission (10-15%) | $200-$500/month stipend + product + commission (15-20%) | $1,000-$3,000/month retainer + product + commission (20-25%) + event access |
| **Product Access** | Seasonal product shipments | Monthly product + early access to new launches | Unlimited product + exclusive pre-launch access + custom items |
| **Brand Support** | Monthly email newsletter, brand guidelines | Dedicated contact, monthly group call, content feedback | Dedicated account manager, 1:1 strategy calls, priority support |
| **Perks** | Brand feature on social, discount code to share | Co-created content opportunities, website feature | Event attendance, product input, co-design collaborations |
| **Exclusivity** | None required | Soft exclusivity (no direct competitors) | Full category exclusivity |
| **Performance Review** | Monthly (auto-renew if meeting minimums) | Quarterly performance review | Quarterly review with annual renewal negotiation |
| **Promotion Path** | Hit 3-month targets --> Growth invitation | Hit 6-month targets --> Elite invitation | Elite Tier + additional negotiated benefits |

### Ambassador Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring (1-5) |
|---|---|---|
| Content quality and aesthetic | 25% | 1=Poor, 3=Good, 5=Exceptional |
| Engagement rate vs. tier average | 20% | 1=Below 50%, 3=At average, 5=2x+ average |
| Audience alignment with brand target | 20% | 1=No match, 3=Moderate, 5=Perfect match |
| Brand affinity (organic mentions, values) | 15% | 1=None, 3=Some alignment, 5=Genuine fan |
| Posting consistency | 10% | 1=Sporadic, 3=Weekly, 5=Daily |
| Professionalism (past partnerships) | 10% | 1=Issues, 3=Adequate, 5=Outstanding |
| **Minimum score to qualify** | | **3.5/5.0 weighted average** |
