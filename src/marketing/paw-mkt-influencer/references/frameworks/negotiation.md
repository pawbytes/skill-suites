# Negotiation Frameworks and Scripts

### The PREP Negotiation Framework

| Step | Action | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **P**repare | Research creator rates, audience data, past partnerships | Check tier benchmarks, engagement rate, content quality |
| **R**ange | Set your budget range (floor, target, ceiling) | Floor: $300, Target: $500, Ceiling: $750 per Reel |
| **E**xchange | Identify value beyond payment to offer | Usage rights, cross-promotion, long-term deal, event access |
| **P**ropose | Lead with mid-range offer and value-adds | "We're thinking $500 per Reel plus product and we'd love to feature your content on our channels" |

### Negotiation Response Scripts

**When a creator's rate is above your budget:**
```
Thanks for sharing your rates, {FirstName}. I really appreciate
the transparency. Your rate of {their rate} is a bit above our
budget for this campaign.

Here's what I can offer:

Option A: {lower rate} per {deliverable} with {value-add:
longer partnership, usage rights included, product, etc.}

Option B: {their rate} per {deliverable} but with a
performance bonus structure: base of {lower rate} plus
{bonus amount} if we hit {metric target}.

Option C: A {number}-month package at {discounted per-post rate}
per deliverable ({total package value}).

Would any of these work for you? Open to discussing what
makes the most sense.
```

**When asked about budget before you've pitched:**
```
Great question! Our budget depends on the scope of the
partnership. To give you an accurate range, it would help
to understand:

- What types of content you'd be most excited to create
- Your typical turnaround time
- Whether you're open to a multi-post or long-term arrangement

Once we align on scope, I can share specific numbers. Our
goal is to create something that feels fair and exciting
for both sides.
```

**When negotiating usage rights:**
```
We'd love to use your content beyond organic posting.
Here's how we typically structure usage rights:

- Organic repost on our channels: Included in base rate
- Paid media license (30 days): +20% of base rate
- Paid media license (90 days): +35% of base rate
- Exclusivity (no competitor posts for {duration}): +50% of base rate

We find most creators prefer the 30-day paid media option
since it keeps overall compensation competitive. What feels
right for you?
```

### Bundling and Discount Strategies

| Bundle Type | Typical Discount | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Multi-post package (3-5 posts) | 10-20% per post | 5 Reels at $450 each vs. $500 each single |
| Long-term contract (3-6 months) | 15-25% per post | 6-month ambassador at $400/Reel vs. $500 one-off |
| Multi-platform package | 10-15% total | Instagram Reel + TikTok video + Stories at 85% of individual rates |
| Usage rights included | 5-10% discount on base | Include 30-day paid media in base rate at slight premium |
| Exclusivity window | No discount; pay premium | Category exclusivity costs 50-100% premium |
