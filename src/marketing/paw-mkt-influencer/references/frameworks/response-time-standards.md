# Response Time Standards

| Stage | Brand Response Time | Creator Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| Initial outreach reply | N/A | 3-7 days (allow grace) |
| Rate/scope discussion | Within 24 hours | Within 48 hours |
| Contract review | Within 48 hours | Within 5 business days |
| Brief questions | Within 24 hours | Within 24 hours |
| Content draft review | Within 24-48 hours | N/A |
| Revision feedback | Within 24 hours | Within 48 hours |
| Final approval | Within 24 hours | N/A |
| Payment processing | Within terms (Net 15/30) | N/A |

### Best Practice: Set expectations in the onboarding process. Include response time commitments in the creative brief and contract outline. Prompt communication builds trust and encourages creators to prioritize your partnership.
