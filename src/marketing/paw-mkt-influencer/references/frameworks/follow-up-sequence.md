# Follow-Up Sequence Framework

### Standard 4-Touch Follow-Up Sequence

| Touchpoint | Timing | Channel | Content Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Touch 1: Initial Outreach | Day 0 | Email or DM | Value proposition with specific content reference (templates above) |
| Touch 2: Gentle Nudge | Day 3-5 | Same channel | Add new value: reference their recent content, share relevant brand news, or mention what other creators are doing with the brand |
| Touch 3: Channel Switch | Day 7-10 | Alternate channel | Short and casual: "Hey {Name}, dropped you a note on email -- wanted to make sure it didn't get lost. Still interested in chatting about a collab?" |
| Touch 4: Door Open | Day 14-21 | Email | No-pressure close: "Totally understand if the timing isn't right. The door is always open if you'd like to collaborate down the road. Keep up the great work." |

### Follow-Up Message Templates

**Touch 2 (Day 3-5):**
```
Hi {FirstName},

Just following up on my note from {day}. I also wanted to
mention -- your {recent post/video from last few days} was
great. {Brief specific comment}.

We've been working with creators like {similar creator or
general description} on {campaign type} and seeing {brief
result}. Would love to explore something similar with you.

Let me know if you'd like to chat!

{YourName}
```

**Touch 3 (Day 7-10, channel switch):**
```
Hey {FirstName}! Sent you an email a few days ago about a
potential collab with {Brand}. Totally get it if you've been
busy -- just wanted to make sure it didn't slip through.

Happy to chat here or via email ({email}).

{YourName} | {Brand}
```

**Touch 4 (Day 14-21, final):**
```
Hi {FirstName},

I know your inbox is probably flooded, so this will be my
last note. I genuinely admire your content and think there's
a great fit with {Brand}.

If the timing ever feels right for a collaboration, the door
is always open. No pressure at all.

Wishing you continued success!

{YourName}
{Title} | {Brand}
```

### Follow-Up Rules

- Never follow up more than 3 times after the initial outreach (4 touches total).
- Every follow-up must add new value. Never send "just checking in" or "following up."
- If a creator does not respond after 4 touches, add them to a "re-engage in 90 days" list.
- If a creator says no, thank them warmly and mark as "declined -- revisit in 6 months."
- If a creator says "not now," schedule a re-engagement outreach for 60-90 days later.
- Track all outreach in a CRM or spreadsheet: creator name, platform, date, response, status.
