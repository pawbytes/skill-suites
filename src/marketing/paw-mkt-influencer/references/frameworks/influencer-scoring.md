# Influencer Scoring Rubric

### Scoring Matrix (Use During Vetting)

| Criterion | Weight | 1 (Poor) | 2 (Below Avg) | 3 (Average) | 4 (Good) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Engagement Rate** | 20% | Below 50% of tier avg | 50-75% of tier avg | At tier average | 1.5x tier avg | 2x+ tier avg |
| **Audience Quality** | 20% | Major mismatch with target | Some overlap | Moderate alignment | Strong alignment | Perfect match (geo, demo, interests) |
| **Content Quality** | 15% | Low effort, inconsistent | Acceptable but generic | Good production, authentic voice | High quality, distinctive style | Exceptional creative, trendsetting |
| **Brand Fit** | 15% | Conflicting values or category | Neutral, no natural connection | General category relevance | Strong category fit, value alignment | Organic fan, natural advocate |
| **Authenticity** | 15% | Red flags (fake followers, bots) | Some questionable signals | Appears genuine, limited data | Strong signals of genuine audience | Verified authentic, community-driven |
| **Posting Consistency** | 5% | Inactive or sporadic | Monthly at best | 2-3x per week | Daily or near-daily | Consistent schedule, high volume |
| **Past Partnerships** | 5% | Compliance issues, poor execution | Limited experience | Adequate, standard executions | Professional, clear disclosures | Outstanding track record, case studies |
| **Professionalism** | 5% | Unresponsive, unreliable | Slow responses, some issues | Responsive, meets deadlines | Proactive communication, on time | Exceeds expectations, adds value |

### Scoring Thresholds

| Score Range | Recommendation | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 4.5-5.0 | Tier 1: Priority partner | Fast-track outreach, offer premium terms |
| 3.5-4.4 | Tier 2: Strong candidate | Standard outreach, competitive offer |
| 2.5-3.4 | Tier 3: Consider for volume | Seed tier or gifting-only partnership |
| Below 2.5 | Pass | Do not pursue; revisit in 6 months |

### Quick-Score Checklist (Rapid Vetting)

Use this 5-question checklist for initial screening before full scoring:

- [ ] Engagement rate at or above tier average?
- [ ] Audience demographics match brand target? (check top countries, age range)
- [ ] Content aesthetic and values align with brand?
- [ ] No red flags? (fake followers, missing disclosures, competitor partnerships)
- [ ] Active and consistent posting in the last 30 days?

If all 5 boxes are checked, proceed to full scoring. If 2+ boxes fail, pass.
