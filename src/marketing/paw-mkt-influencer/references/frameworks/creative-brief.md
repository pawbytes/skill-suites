# Creative Brief Template

```markdown
# Creator Brief: {Campaign Name}
## Date: {YYYY-MM-DD}
## Brand: {Brand Name}

---

### Brand Overview
{2-3 sentences: what the brand is, who it serves, what makes it different}

### Campaign Objective
{Primary goal: awareness / traffic / conversions / UGC generation / product launch}
KPI: {specific metric and target, e.g., "50,000 video views" or "200 link clicks"}

### Target Audience
{Who we're trying to reach: demographics, interests, pain points}

### Key Messages (pick 2-3 max)
1. {Message 1: the primary takeaway}
2. {Message 2: supporting proof point}
3. {Message 3: differentiator or CTA}

### Content Requirements
- Platform: {Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube / etc.}
- Format: {vertical video / carousel / Story set / etc.}
- Length: {15-60 seconds / 3-5 carousel slides / etc.}
- Deliverables: {1 Reel + 3 Stories / 2 TikTok videos / etc.}

### Must-Include Elements
- [ ] Product shown in use (not just held up)
- [ ] Brand @handle tagged: {@brandhandle}
- [ ] Campaign hashtag: {#CampaignHashtag}
- [ ] Disclosure: #ad or platform paid partnership label
- [ ] Tracking link/code: {unique link or discount code}
- [ ] CTA: {specific call to action, e.g., "Link in bio to shop"}

### Creative Freedom (what YOU decide)
- Setting and backdrop
- Script and speaking style
- Hook and opening
- Music selection
- Personal anecdote or story

### Do NOT Include
- {Competitor mentions or products visible}
- {Specific claims we can't make: medical, guaranteed results, etc.}
- {Off-brand language, themes, or settings}

### Timeline
| Milestone | Date |
|---|---|
| Brief sent to creator | {date} |
| Content draft due | {date} |
| Brand feedback by | {date} |
| Revised draft due (if needed) | {date} |
| Final approval confirmed | {date} |
| Publish window opens | {date} |
| Publish window closes | {date} |

### Compensation & Usage Rights
- Fee: {amount} per {deliverable}
- Product: {what they receive}
- Usage rights: {organic only / 30-day paid media / full buyout}
- Payment: {Net 15 / Net 30 / upon content approval}
- Payment method: {PayPal / bank transfer / platform}

### Brand Assets
- Logo files: {link to drive/folder}
- Brand guidelines: {link}
- Product images: {link}
- Approved messaging: {link to this brief}

### Point of Contact
- Name: {YourName}
- Email: {email}
- Response time: Within 24 hours
```
