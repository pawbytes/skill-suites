# Performance Measurement

> Capability prompt for paw-mkt-influencer skill. Load when user needs metrics, tracking setup, benchmarks, or campaign reporting.

---

## Core Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| CPM | (Cost / impressions) x 1,000 | Awareness efficiency |
| CPE | Cost / engagements | Engagement efficiency |
| CPA | Cost / conversions | Conversion efficiency |
| Engagement Rate | (Likes + comments + shares + saves) / reach x 100 | Content resonance |
| Earned Media Value | Impressions x industry CPM benchmark | Estimated equivalent ad spend |
| ROAS | Revenue / cost | Return on investment |

## Tracking Methods

| Method | Accuracy | Coverage | Ease of Setup |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unique discount codes | High | 30-50% of conversions | Easy |
| UTM-tagged links (GA4) | High | 20-40% of conversions | Medium |
| Affiliate platform tracking | High | 40-60% of conversions | Medium |
| Post-purchase surveys | Medium-High | 15-30% of conversions | Easy |
| Brand search lift | Medium | Indirect measurement | Complex |
| Platform-native insights | Medium | Platform-specific only | Easy |
| Unique landing pages | High | Per-creator tracking | Medium |

## Attribution Reality

Influencer marketing is hard to attribute -- expect 20-40% of impact to be unmeasurable via direct response.

Use combination of methods:
- Discount codes (first-touch)
- UTM links (click-through)
- Brand search lift
- Post-purchase surveys
- Revenue correlation analysis
