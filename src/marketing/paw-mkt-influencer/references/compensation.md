## Compensation Models

| Model | Structure | Best For | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gifting | Free product, no payment | Nano creators, seeding | Low cost, no guaranteed output |
| Flat Fee | Fixed per deliverable | Sponsored posts | Predictable cost, no performance alignment |
| Performance (CPA/CPC) | Pay per conversion or click | Affiliate, conversion-focused | Low risk, requires tracking |
| Revenue Share | % of sales generated | Long-term partnerships, collabs | Aligned incentives, variable cost |
| Hybrid | Base fee + performance bonus | Ambassador programs | Moderate risk, aligned incentives |
| Equity | Ownership stake | Startup partnerships | Long-term alignment, complex, rare |

## Pricing Guidelines

### Instagram
- Post: $100-$500 per 10K followers
- Reels premium: +50-100% over static posts
- Story set (3-5 frames): 30-50% of feed post rate

### TikTok
- Video: $200-$800 per 10K followers
- TikTok Shop: Base fee + commission (10-25%)

### YouTube
- Integration: $500-$2,000 per 10K subscribers
- Dedicated video: 2-3x integration pricing
- Shorts: $50-$250 per 10K subscribers

### LinkedIn
- Post: $100-$300 per 10K followers
- Article: $200-$500 per 10K followers
- Video: $200-$600 per 10K followers

## Usage Rights Pricing

| Rights Level | Additional Cost | Duration | Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organic repost only | Included or +10% | Campaign duration | Brand social channels |
| Paid media license | +15-30% | 30-90 days | Running as paid ads |
| Extended paid media | +30-50% | 6-12 months | Long-term ad campaigns |
| Exclusivity (category) | +50-100% | Contract duration | No competitor partnerships |
| Full buyout (perpetuity) | +100-200% | Unlimited | All channels, all uses, forever |
| Whitelisting/Spark Ads | +15-25% | 30-60 days | Running ads from creator's account |

## Negotiation Tactics

1. **Lead with value**: Highlight what the creator gets beyond payment -- audience growth, content assets, exclusive access, long-term relationship.
2. **Know your ceiling**: Set maximum cost per creator before negotiation. Anchor at mid-range.
3. **Bundle for discounts**: Multi-post packages, long-term contracts, exclusivity windows reduce per-deliverable cost.
4. **Performance bonuses**: Base pay plus bonus for exceeding targets aligns incentives.
5. **Usage rights**: Negotiate upfront. Organic-only costs less than paid media licensing.
6. **Creative control**: Give freedom within guardrails. The best briefs define outcomes, not scripts.

For detailed negotiation scripts, read `./references/frameworks/negotiation.md`.

## Budget Rule

80% of budget on nano/micro creators delivers more total engagement than 80% on one macro creator. 

Example allocation for $10,000 budget:
- 70% ($7,000): 20-30 nano/micro creators ($100-$350 each)
- 20% ($2,000): 2-3 mid-tier creators ($500-$1,000 each)
- 10% ($1,000): Paid amplification of top-performing content
