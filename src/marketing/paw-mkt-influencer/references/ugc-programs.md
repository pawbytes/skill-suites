# UGC Programs

> Capability prompt for paw-mkt-influencer skill. Load when user needs UGC collection, rights management, or repurposing strategy.

---

## Collecting UGC

### Branded Hashtags
Create a unique hashtag. Promote on packaging, website, email, social bios. Feature best submissions.

### Post-Purchase Prompts
Email 7-14 days after delivery requesting content with branded hashtag or tag.

### Contests and Challenges
Launch a challenge with theme, mechanics, and prize. Clear rules, simple entry, shareable format.

### In-Product Prompts
In-app or on-site prompts inviting users to share results or testimonials.

### Hired UGC Creators
Pay creators ($150-$500/video) to produce UGC-style content for brand channels and ads. UGC content outperforms polished brand creative by 2-4x on Meta and TikTok paid ads.

## Rights Management

### Permission Levels

| Level | Scope | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| Organic repost | Credit creator, brand social only | Social feeds, Stories |
| Paid media license | Ads for defined period and platforms | Meta/TikTok ads, whitelisting |
| Full buyout | Unlimited usage, all channels, perpetuity | Website, retail, packaging |

### Documentation Requirements

Always get explicit written permission before reposting. Specify in writing:
- Usage scope
- Duration
- Platforms
- Editing permissions

### UGC Library Organization

Organize by:
- Content type
- Product
- Platform
- Rights level
- Expiration date

| Incentive | Cost | Effectiveness |
|---|---|---|

| Loyalty points | Program-dependent | Moderate motivation |

| Cash rewards | Variable | High motivation |

## Repurposing UGC

### Organic Social
- Repost with credit
- Carousel compilations
- Story highlights

### Paid Ads
- UGC-style outperforms polished creative 2-4x on Meta and TikTok
- Negotiate paid media rights upfront
- Refresh creative every 2-4 weeks

### Website
- Product pages
- Testimonials sections
- Landing pages

### Email
- Customer spotlights
- Social proof sections

### Retail and Packaging
- Requires full buyout rights
- In-store displays
- Product packaging

## UGC Creator Networks

- Brands maintaining a roster of 10-20 UGC creators can produce 50-100+ ad variations per month for creative testing.
- Provide clear ad briefs (hook, product demo, CTA).
- Request raw footage plus edited versions.
- Negotiate full usage rights upfront.
- Maintain rotating roster for creative diversity.
