# Platform Migration Playbook

## When to Migrate

Reasons to consider migration:
- Platform no longer supports community size or needs
- Cost has become prohibitive
- Critical features are missing (courses, monetization, branding)
- Data ownership concerns
- Platform announced deprecation or major changes

## Migration Timeline (8-12 Weeks)

| Phase | Duration | Actions |
|---|---|---|
| Assessment | Week 1-2 | Evaluate new platform options, test top 2-3 candidates, check data export capabilities |
| Decision | Week 3 | Select new platform, secure budget approval, announce to moderator team |
| Setup | Week 4-5 | Configure new platform, migrate content (where possible), set up structure |
| Beta migration | Week 6-7 | Move moderators and champions to new platform first, test all workflows |
| Announcement | Week 8 | Announce migration to full community, explain why, set timeline |
| Parallel run | Week 9-10 | Run both platforms simultaneously, direct all new activity to new platform |
| Full migration | Week 11 | Make old platform read-only, final member migration push |
| Cleanup | Week 12 | Archive old platform, resolve migration issues, gather feedback |

## Migration Communication Template

**Announcement message**:
"We are moving [community name] to [new platform] starting [date]. Here is why: [2-3 bullet points on benefits for members]. Here is what you need to do: [specific steps with link]. Here is the timeline: [key dates]. Your content and history matter to us -- here is how we are preserving it: [migration details]. Questions? Ask in [dedicated thread]."

## Migration Risks and Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Member loss (10-30% typical) | Communicate early and often, make migration easy, offer incentives for early movers |
| Content loss | Export everything possible before migration, screenshot or archive what cannot be exported |
| Engagement dip (4-8 weeks post-migration) | Plan intensive programming during transition, extra events, contests, re-engagement campaigns |
| Technical issues | Beta test thoroughly, have support ready, maintain old platform as read-only backup |
| Community culture shift | Move moderators and champions first to establish culture on new platform before general population |
