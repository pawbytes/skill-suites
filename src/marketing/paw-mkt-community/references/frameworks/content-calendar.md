# Content Calendar

## Daily Themes Template

| Day | Theme | Content Type | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Goals and Planning | Discussion prompt | "What is your top priority this week?" |
| Tuesday | Tips and Resources | Expert content | Team-curated tip, tool recommendation, or resource share |
| Wednesday | Community Event | Live engagement | Voice chat, workshop, AMA, co-working session |
| Thursday | Discussion and Debate | Opinion prompt | "Unpopular opinion: [take]. Agree or disagree?" |
| Friday | Wins and Celebrations | Community celebration | "Share your biggest win this week, no matter how small." |
| Saturday | (Optional) Casual | Off-topic or lifestyle | "What are you reading/watching/learning this weekend?" |
| Sunday | Rest | No scheduled content | Let organic conversation happen |

## Weekly Features

| Feature | Cadence | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Discussion prompt rotation | Daily (M-F) | Rotate between opinion, experience, advice, resource, and debate prompts |
| Community event | Weekly (fixed day) | Consistent day and time. Alternate formats: casual, structured, guest |
| Member spotlight | Weekly or biweekly | Featured interview with a standout member |
| Resource drop | Weekly | Curated tool, template, framework, or guide |
| Community digest | Weekly | Summary of top discussions, upcoming events, and highlights |

## Monthly Events Calendar

| Week | Event Type | Format | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Monthly theme launch + challenge kickoff | Announcement + challenge post | Async |
| Week 2 | Guest expert session or external AMA | Live video/audio (recorded) | 45-60 min |
| Week 3 | Member spotlight + mid-month check-in | Published feature + discussion | Async |
| Week 4 | Monthly roundup + next month preview | Recap post + survey | Async |

## Quarterly Programming

| Quarter Milestone | Description |
|---|---|
| Quarterly report | Full metrics review, health score, business impact analysis |
| Quarterly guest expert event | High-profile speaker, panel, or workshop |
| Quarterly challenge | Larger-scale challenge (30-day format) |
| Quarterly member awards | Recognize top contributors, most helpful, most improved |
| Quarterly strategy review | Evaluate what is working, adjust programming, update targets |
