# 90-Day Launch Plan

Nearly 70% of online communities fail in their first year (Gartner). The primary causes are unclear goals, no strategic alignment, and inconsistent execution. This playbook provides a week-by-week plan to avoid those failures.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

**Week 1: Strategic Foundation**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Define community purpose (1-2 sentences) | Community lead | Community promise statement |
| Identify primary community type (customer, industry, interest, practice) | Community lead + leadership | Type decision documented |
| Define target member persona (who are the first 50 people?) | Community lead + marketing | Member persona document |
| Set 90-day success metrics and targets | Community lead | Metrics dashboard setup |
| Audit existing audience touchpoints (email list, social, customers) | Marketing team | Audience inventory with sizes |
| Review brand SOSTAC plan for community alignment | Community lead | Alignment notes |

**Week 2: Platform and Infrastructure**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Select community platform (see platform decision framework) | Community lead | Platform decision rationale |
| Set up platform account with channel/space structure | Community lead | Configured platform |
| Configure roles, permissions, and access tiers | Community lead | Roles and permissions document |
| Set up moderation tools (bots, filters, reporting) | Community lead | Moderation tools configured |
| Write community guidelines and code of conduct | Community lead | Published guidelines |
| Design onboarding flow (welcome message, intro prompt, first action) | Community lead | Onboarding sequence documented |

**Week 3: Content Seeding and Recruitment Prep**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Write 10-15 seed discussion posts (varied categories) | Content team | Pre-written posts scheduled |
| Create 3-5 resource threads (guides, FAQs, tools) | Content team | Published resource threads |
| Write welcome guide for new members | Community lead | Welcome guide published |
| Build founding member target list (50-100 names) | Community lead + sales/CS | Prioritized list with outreach channel |
| Draft founding member invitation message | Community lead | Personalized invite template |
| Create community landing page or waitlist | Marketing team | Live landing page |

**Week 4: Founding Member Recruitment**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Send personal invitations to founding member list | Community lead | 50-100 invitations sent |
| Follow up on non-responses after 3-5 days | Community lead | Follow-up messages sent |
| Onboard first wave of founding members (target: 20-50) | Community lead | Founding members active |
| Conduct welcome calls or DMs with each founding member | Community lead | Personal connections established |
| Gather founding member feedback on platform and structure | Community lead | Feedback log |
| Assign "Founding Member" badges or roles | Community lead | Badges assigned |

## Phase 2: Soft Launch (Days 31-60)

**Week 5: Beta Community Operations**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Run community in soft launch with founding members only | Community lead | Active beta community |
| Post 3-5 discussion prompts per week | Community lead | Regular discussion cadence |
| Host first community event (AMA, casual hangout, or workshop) | Community lead | Event completed, feedback collected |
| Test all onboarding workflows with real members | Community lead | Workflow refinements documented |
| Identify 3-5 natural community champions from active members | Community lead | Champion candidates list |
| Refine channel/space structure based on actual usage | Community lead | Updated structure |

**Week 6: Prepare for Public Launch**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Finalize onboarding flow based on beta feedback | Community lead | Polished onboarding sequence |
| Appoint volunteer moderators from founding members (2-3) | Community lead | Moderators trained and appointed |
| Create launch week content calendar (daily activities for 7 days) | Community lead + content | Launch week plan |
| Write launch announcement copy (email, social, in-product, website) | Marketing team | Approved announcement copy |
| Plan launch event (AMA, challenge kickoff, or live session) | Community lead | Event logistics confirmed |
| Brief founding members on launch plans; ask them to welcome new members | Community lead | Founding members briefed |

**Week 7: Public Launch**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Publish launch announcements across all channels simultaneously | Marketing team | Announcements live |
| Open community to public with launch event | Community lead | Community publicly open |
| Run 48-hour welcome challenge for all new members | Community lead | Challenge active |
| Monitor onboarding flow and fix bottlenecks in real-time | Community lead | Daily optimization notes |
| Respond to every new member introduction within 4 hours | Community lead + moderators | 100% response rate target |
| Track daily: new joins, intro posts, event attendance, questions | Community lead | Daily metrics log |

**Week 8: Post-Launch Stabilization**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Analyze launch week metrics (growth, activation, engagement) | Community lead | Launch week report |
| Identify and address onboarding drop-off points | Community lead | Onboarding fixes implemented |
| Continue daily discussion prompts and content seeding | Community lead | Consistent daily activity |
| Host second community event (different format from launch event) | Community lead | Event completed |
| Send personal check-ins to members who joined but have not posted | Community lead | Re-engagement outreach |
| Publish first "community highlights" recap | Community lead | Highlights post published |

## Phase 3: Growth and Optimization (Days 61-90)

**Week 9-10: Establish Rhythms**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Establish weekly engagement rhythm (Monday goals, Wednesday event, Friday wins) | Community lead | Weekly rhythm operational |
| Launch first monthly challenge or program | Community lead | Challenge launched |
| Create first member spotlight feature | Community lead | Member spotlight published |
| Begin repurposing community content for external channels | Marketing team | First external content piece |
| Evaluate and adjust moderation approach based on 30 days of data | Community lead | Moderation refinements |
| Start tracking community health score weekly | Community lead | Health score dashboard active |

**Week 11-12: Scale and Plan Forward**
| Action | Owner | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Compile 90-day community report (metrics, learnings, recommendations) | Community lead | 90-day report delivered to leadership |
| Recruit additional volunteer moderators if needed | Community lead | Moderator team scaled |
| Plan quarter two programming (events, challenges, guest experts) | Community lead | Q2 content calendar |
| Evaluate staffing needs: does community need more dedicated resources? | Community lead + leadership | Staffing recommendation |
| Assess platform fit: is the platform still the right choice? | Community lead | Platform evaluation |
| Set quarter two targets and OKRs | Community lead | Q2 targets documented |
