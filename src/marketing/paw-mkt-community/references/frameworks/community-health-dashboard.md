# Community Health Dashboard

## Weekly Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| New members | Count of joins this week | Positive trend | Platform analytics |
| Churned members | Count of leaves or inactive (30 days) | Below 5% weekly | Platform analytics + manual |
| Net growth | New - Churned | Positive | Calculated |
| Daily active members (avg) | Sum of daily actives / 7 | 20-30% of total | Platform analytics |
| Posts created | Total new posts this week | Stable or growing | Platform analytics |
| Comments and replies | Total replies this week | 3-8 replies per post avg | Platform analytics |
| Questions asked | Count of question-type posts | Stable | Manual tag or platform |
| Questions answered (<24h) | Questions with a reply within 24 hours | 80%+ | Manual tracking |
| Events held | Count of events this week | 1+ per week | Calendar |
| Event attendance | Avg attendees per event | 30-50% of RSVPs | Event platform |
| Moderation actions | Warnings + mutes + bans | Low and stable | Moderation log |
| Health score | Composite (see scoring model) | 60+ | Calculated weekly |
| Sentiment | Qualitative assessment or AI sentiment | Positive dominant | Manual or AI tool |

## Monthly Business Impact Metrics

| Metric | Measurement | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Support tickets deflected | Questions resolved in community that would have been tickets | 20-40% deflection |
| NPS (community members) | NPS survey of community members | 50+ |
| Community-attributed signups | New product signups from community members or referrals | Tracked and growing |
| Content pieces repurposed | Community content turned into blog, social, email content | 4+ per month |
| Member referrals | New members from existing member referrals | 10-20% of new joins |
| Revenue attribution | Revenue from community members vs. non-members | 2-4x higher LTV |
