# Ambassador and Champion Program Design

## Three-Tier Framework

Structured ambassador programs with clear tiers show 33% better tracking accuracy and double the conversion rates of unstructured programs (Shopify/Roster research).

**Tier 1: Supporters (Entry Level)**
| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| Criteria to enter | Active for 30+ days, 10+ posts or comments, positive engagement history |
| Responsibilities | Welcome new members, answer basic questions, share community content externally |
| Rewards | Supporter badge, recognition in community, early access to new content |
| Number of people | Unlimited (anyone who qualifies) |
| Progression | Automatic qualification based on activity metrics |

**Tier 2: Champions (Mid Level)**
| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| Criteria to enter | Supporter for 60+ days, 50+ posts/comments, hosted or co-hosted an event, referred 3+ members |
| Responsibilities | Lead discussions, moderate threads, mentor new members, create original content, represent community at events |
| Rewards | Champion badge, direct team access, swag, exclusive channel/space, input on community direction |
| Number of people | 10-30 (curated, application-based) |
| Progression | Application + review by community team |

**Tier 3: Ambassadors (Top Level)**
| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| Criteria to enter | Champion for 90+ days, created 5+ content pieces, referred 10+ members, demonstrated leadership |
| Responsibilities | Speak on behalf of community externally, create case studies, co-create content with team, advise on strategy, recruit champions |
| Rewards | Ambassador title, co-creation opportunities, annual summit invitation, product input, revenue share or compensation |
| Number of people | 5-15 (highly selective, invitation-based) |
| Progression | Invitation from community team based on track record |

## Ambassador Program Operations

- **Monthly ambassador sync**: 30-minute video call with all ambassadors. Share updates, gather feedback, align on priorities.
- **Quarterly ambassador review**: Evaluate each ambassador's activity, impact, and tier status. Promote, maintain, or (rarely) off-board.
- **Ambassador toolkit**: Brand guidelines, content templates, talking points, referral links, swag kit.
- **Recognition calendar**: Public recognition monthly (social shoutout, community spotlight). Private recognition quarterly (personal thank you from leadership).
