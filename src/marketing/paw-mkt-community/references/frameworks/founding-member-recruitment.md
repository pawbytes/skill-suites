# Founding Member Recruitment Playbook

## Who to Recruit

Founding members set the culture, norms, and tone for the entire community. They are the most important community decision.

**Ideal founding member sources** (in priority order):
1. Power users or top customers who already engage with your product actively
2. Vocal customers who give unsolicited feedback (positive or constructive)
3. Social media followers who comment and engage regularly
4. Email subscribers who reply to newsletters
5. Beta testers from previous product launches
6. Industry contacts who share your target audience
7. Existing network connections who match the member persona

**Founding member qualities to look for**:
- Already active in relevant online spaces (proves willingness to engage)
- Shares or creates content related to your topic area
- Has given you feedback before (shows investment in the relationship)
- Has a track record of being constructive and collaborative
- Represents the diversity of your target audience

## Outreach Strategy

**Personal invitation (highest conversion, 40-60%)**:
- Individual email or DM, not a mass blast
- Reference a specific interaction or contribution they have made
- Explain what makes them specifically valuable as a founding member
- Template: "Hi [Name], I am building [community name] for [audience] to [value proposition]. I thought of you because [specific reason]. As a founding member, you would get [benefits]. Would you like to join the first group? I am inviting just [number] people to start."

**Waitlist application (medium conversion, 15-30%)**:
- Landing page with community promise and founding member benefits
- Short application form (3-5 questions: who are you, why join, what will you contribute)
- Creates scarcity and selectivity signals
- Review applications and send personal acceptance messages

**Existing channel promotion (lower conversion, 5-15%)**:
- Newsletter announcement with exclusive founding member offer
- Social media posts teasing the community with an application link
- In-product notification for eligible users
- Less personal, but broader reach

## Founding Member Benefits

| Benefit | Why It Works |
|---|---|
| "Founding Member" title/badge (permanent) | Identity and status -- visible recognition that persists |
| Direct input on community direction | Ownership and voice -- members feel invested |
| Early access to features, content, or events | Exclusivity -- tangible value for being first |
| Direct access to the team (founders, product, leadership) | Proximity to power -- high-value perk for engaged members |
| Locked-in pricing (if paid) at 20-40% discount forever | Financial incentive with long-term lock-in |
| Public recognition as the community grows | Status that compounds -- becomes more valuable over time |
