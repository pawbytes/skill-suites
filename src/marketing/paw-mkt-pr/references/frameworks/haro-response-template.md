# HARO / Source Request Response Template

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

```
SUBJECT: Re: [Exact query subject line from the platform]

Hi [Journalist First Name],

[CREDENTIALS -- 1-2 sentences]
I'm [Full Name], [Title] at [Company]. I've [specific experience relevant to this
query: years in the field, published research, direct experience, client work,
certifications].

[DIRECT ANSWER -- 2-4 quotable sentences]
[Answer the journalist's exact question directly. Write in a voice that can be
copied verbatim into an article. Be specific, not generic. Include a data point
or concrete example if possible.]

[UNIQUE ANGLE -- 1-2 sentences]
[Add something the journalist likely has not heard from other respondents: a
contrarian perspective, a proprietary data point, a specific first-hand example,
or a forward-looking prediction.]

[ASSETS]
- Headshot: [Link to high-resolution headshot]
- Bio: [2-3 sentence professional bio]
- Website: [URL]

Happy to expand on any of this or provide additional context.

Best,
[Full Name]
[Title], [Company]
[Email] | [Phone]
```

### HARO Response Rules

- Respond within 1-2 hours. Speed is the single biggest success factor.
- Keep the entire response under 200 words (excluding bio and assets).
- Answer the specific question asked. Do not pitch your product.
- Write in quotable sentences. Make it easy to copy into an article.
- Always include headshot and bio. Journalists skip responses without them.
- Do not respond to queries outside your genuine expertise.
- Track every response: date, platform, query topic, journalist, outcome.
