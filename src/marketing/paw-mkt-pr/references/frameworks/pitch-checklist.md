# Quick Reference: Pitch Checklist

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

Before sending any pitch, verify:

- [ ] Subject line is under 10 words and leads with the news hook
- [ ] Opening sentence references the journalist's recent work
- [ ] The story is clear in the first 2 sentences
- [ ] Specific data or facts are included (not just claims)
- [ ] There is a clear, single offer (exclusive, interview, data, demo)
- [ ] Total pitch is under 200 words
- [ ] No attachments (links to assets instead)
- [ ] Sent from a personal email, not a generic inbox
- [ ] Sent Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM in journalist's timezone
- [ ] Journalist's beat matches the story
- [ ] Follow-up plan is scheduled (Day 3, Day 7)
