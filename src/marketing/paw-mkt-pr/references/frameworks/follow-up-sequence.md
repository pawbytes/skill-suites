# Follow-Up Sequence

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

Never send "just checking in" follow-ups. Every touchpoint must add new value.

### Day 0: Initial Pitch

Send the full pitch per the templates in the pitch-types framework. This is the only email that
contains the complete story.

### Day 3: Follow-Up 1 -- New Angle

```
SUBJECT: Re: [Original subject line]

Hi [First Name],

Quick follow-up on [topic]. Since I reached out, [new development]:

- [New data point, angle, or timely hook that was not in the original pitch]

[One sentence restating the core offer]. Let me know if this is a fit.

Best,
[Name]
```

Purpose: Add a genuinely new piece of information -- a fresh data point, a new angle
connected to breaking news, an additional expert who is now available, or updated
numbers.

### Day 7: Follow-Up 2 -- Timely Hook

```
SUBJECT: Re: [Original subject line]

Hi [First Name],

Saw your recent piece on [their recent article]. [Brief genuine comment.]

Our [data/expert/story] ties directly into this -- [one sentence connecting your
pitch to their recent coverage].

Happy to share [specific asset] if helpful for a follow-up piece. No worries if
the timing doesn't work.

Best,
[Name]
```

Purpose: Connect your pitch to something the journalist just published or a current
news event. This demonstrates you are paying attention and the story is timely.

### Day 14: Move On

Do not send a third follow-up on the same pitch. If there has been no response after
two follow-ups:

- Mark the journalist as "pitched, no response" in your tracker.
- Wait at least 4-6 weeks before pitching the same journalist on a different story.
- Consider whether the pitch simply was not a fit for their beat.
- Keep engaging with their work on social media to maintain visibility.

### Follow-Up Rules

- Always reply to the original thread (same subject line with Re:).
- Space follow-ups 3-4 days apart. Shorter gaps feel pushy.
- Each follow-up should be shorter than the previous one.
- If they respond with "not right now," thank them and move on.
- If they respond with interest, reply within 1 hour with everything they need.
- Track all follow-ups in your outreach CRM (BuzzStream, Pitchbox, or spreadsheet).
