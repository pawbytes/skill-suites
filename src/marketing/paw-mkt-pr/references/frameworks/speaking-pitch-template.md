# Speaking Pitch Template

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

```
SUBJECT: Speaker proposal: [Talk Title] for [Event Name]

Hi [Organizer First Name],

I'm writing to propose a session for [Event Name / Conference] on [Date/Year]:

**Talk title**: [Specific, compelling title -- not generic]

**Description**: [2-3 sentences. What the audience will learn and why it matters
now. Focus on takeaways, not the speaker's background.]

**Key takeaways for attendees**:
1. [Specific, actionable takeaway #1]
2. [Specific, actionable takeaway #2]
3. [Specific, actionable takeaway #3]

**Format**: [Keynote / Panel / Workshop / Fireside chat]. [Duration] minutes.
Happy to adapt to what works best for the program.

**About me**: [3-4 sentences. Title, credentials, and why this person is qualified
to deliver this talk. Include relevant metrics, publications, or experience.]

**Previous speaking**: [List 2-3 recent events. Link to video if available.]

I can customize this talk to align with [Event Name]'s theme of [theme if known].
Happy to discuss further.

Best,
[Name]
[Title], [Company]
[Website] | [LinkedIn]
```

### Speaking Pitch Tips

- Apply 6-12 months in advance for major conferences.
- Propose a specific talk, not "I'd love to speak at your event."
- Focus on audience value, not self-promotion.
- Include video evidence of speaking ability when available.
- Offer to customize for the event's theme and audience.
- Follow up once after 2 weeks if no response.
