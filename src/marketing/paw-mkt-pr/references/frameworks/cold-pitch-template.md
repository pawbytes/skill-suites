# Cold Pitch Email Template

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

The universal structure that underlies all pitch types. Every pitch should contain
these five elements regardless of the specific format.

### Structure

```
SUBJECT LINE: [6-10 words. News hook, not company name. Under 36 characters ideal.]

Hi [First Name],

[PERSONALIZED OPENING -- 1-2 sentences referencing their recent work]
I read your piece on [specific article title/topic] in [Publication] -- [specific,
genuine observation about the piece, not generic flattery].

[THE STORY -- 2-3 sentences with the core news hook]
[What is the news? Why does it matter? Why now?]

[KEY DETAILS -- 2-4 bullet points with specifics]
- [Data point, finding, or fact #1]
- [Data point, finding, or fact #2]
- [Data point, finding, or fact #3]

[THE OFFER -- 1-2 sentences]
I can provide [exclusive data / an interview with our CEO / pre-release access /
expert commentary / high-res visuals]. Happy to share more details or jump on a
quick call.

Best,
[Name]
[Title], [Company]
[Email] | [Phone]
```

### Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| New data: [surprising finding] | New data: 67% of remote workers skip lunch |
| Exclusive: [newsworthy claim] | Exclusive: Series B closes at $45M valuation |
| Expert source: [trending topic] | Expert source: AI hiring bias debate |
| Trend: [pattern] + data available | Trend: Gen Z quitting jobs faster -- data inside |
| [Brand] launches [product solving X] | Acme launches AI tool cutting hiring time 60% |
| [Number] + [surprising finding] | 3 in 4 CFOs expect recession by Q3 |

### Key Rules

- Keep total pitch under 200 words (not counting signature).
- No attachments on first contact. Link to assets or embed in body.
- Send from a personal email address, not a generic press@ inbox.
- Write in plain text or minimal HTML. Heavy formatting triggers spam filters.
- Include a clear, single ask. Do not offer five different story angles in one email.
