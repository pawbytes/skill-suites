# Press Release Template

> Part of the **Media Pitch Templates & Frameworks** collection.
> For the full index of all pitch frameworks, see `./references/frameworks-index.csv`.

```markdown
# [HEADLINE: Active voice, newsworthy, under 80 characters]
## [Subheadline: Expands with a key detail or context]

**[CITY, State] -- [YYYY-MM-DD]** --

[Lead paragraph: Who, what, when, where, why in 2-3 sentences. The single most
important information. If someone reads only this paragraph, they should
understand the news.]

[Second paragraph: Supporting detail, context, significance. Why does this matter
to the reader? What is the broader impact?]

"[Quote from company spokesperson -- adds opinion, vision, or personality. Never
restates facts already in the release.]" -- [Full Name], [Title], [Company].

[Body paragraph 1: Additional details, data, background. Specifics and numbers.]

[Body paragraph 2: Customer/partner/analyst context. Social proof or third-party
validation.]

[Optional second quote from customer, partner, or industry analyst.]

[CTA paragraph: Where to learn more, sign up, or get involved. Include URL.]

### About [Company Name]
[Boilerplate: 2-3 sentences. What the company does, when founded, key metrics
(customers, team size, funding), mission. Update quarterly.]

### Media Contact
[Name] | [Email] | [Phone]
[Response time commitment, e.g., "Responses within 2 hours during business hours"]
```

### Press Release Guidelines

- **400-600 words** total. Shorter is better.
- **Inverted pyramid**: Most important information first. Each paragraph should stand
  alone if the rest were cut.
- **Newsworthiness test**: Funding rounds, major partnerships, original data, significant
  milestones qualify. Internal promotions and minor features do not.
- **Quotes add insight**: Quotes should provide opinion, vision, or personality that
  a journalist cannot write themselves. Never restate facts.
- **Data and specifics**: Concrete numbers outperform vague superlatives.
- **Modern enhancements**: Embed or link to high-res images, video, infographics.
  Visual assets increase pickup 2-3x. Include 2-3 social-ready pull quotes.
- **SEO optimization**: Include target keywords naturally in the headline and first
  paragraph. Structure for AI parsability with clear entity names and data points.
