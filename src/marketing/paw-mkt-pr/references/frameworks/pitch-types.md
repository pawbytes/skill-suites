### 3.1 News Pitch (Product Launch / Company News)

```
SUBJECT: [Brand] launches [product] -- [key benefit in numbers]

Hi [First Name],

Your coverage of [related topic/competitor] in [Publication] caught my eye --
particularly [specific detail].

[Brand] just launched [Product], which [one sentence: what it does and why it
matters to the journalist's audience].

Key details:
- [Differentiator #1 with specific metric]
- [Differentiator #2 with social proof or data]
- [Availability, pricing, or access details]

I can offer [demo access / founder interview / press kit with images and specs].
Would a [5-minute call / press kit] be useful?

Best,
[Name]
```

### 3.2 Expert Commentary Pitch

```
SUBJECT: Expert source: [trending topic]

Hi [First Name],

Your recent piece on [specific article] raised important questions about [topic].

[Expert Name], [Title] at [Company], has [specific credential: years of experience,
relevant background, published research]. [He/She/They] can provide [expert
commentary / data-backed perspective / contrarian insight] on:

- "[One quotable sentence that demonstrates the quality of commentary]"
- [Second specific angle they can speak to]

[Expert Name] is available for a [phone call / written quote / video interview]
on deadline. I can also send a full written response within [timeframe].

Best,
[Name]
```

### 3.3 Data Study Pitch

```
SUBJECT: New data: [most surprising finding with number]

Hi [First Name],

Given your coverage of [beat/topic], I thought you'd find this relevant.

We [surveyed X people / analyzed X data points / compiled X public records] and
found [headline finding].

Top findings:
- [Finding #1 with specific percentage or number]
- [Finding #2 with specific percentage or number]
- [Finding #3 with specific percentage or number]

Full methodology: [sample size, margin of error, timeframe]. I can provide the
complete data set, visualizations, and expert commentary from [Spokesperson Name,
Title].

[Optional: Would you like exclusive access before the public release on [date]?]

Best,
[Name]
```

### 3.4 Trend Piece Pitch

```
SUBJECT: Trend: [pattern] -- data and expert available

Hi [First Name],

I've been following your reporting on [beat] and noticed you've covered [related
trend] recently.

We're seeing [describe the trend in one sentence], supported by:
- [Data point #1 -- from your data or third-party research]
- [Data point #2]
- [Anecdotal evidence or customer insight]

[Expert Name], [Title] at [Company], has been [tracking this / working on solutions
for this / researching this] for [timeframe] and can provide [data-backed
commentary / customer case studies / predictions].

Would this fit a story you're working on? Happy to connect you directly.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 3.5 Exclusive Pitch

```
SUBJECT: Exclusive: [newsworthy claim -- specific and compelling]

Hi [First Name],

I'm reaching out with an exclusive for [Publication] because [specific reason this
fits their audience/beat].

[One paragraph: the news, why it matters, key numbers. This should be strong enough
to convince the journalist to take the exclusive.]

Key details:
- [Most important fact]
- [Second most important fact]
- [Scale / impact / what makes this significant]

We're offering this exclusively to [Publication] with a [date] embargo. If
[Journalist First Name] is interested, I can provide [full press kit / data /
interview access / advance product access] immediately.

Please let me know by [deadline -- typically 48-72 hours] so we can finalize
our distribution plan.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 3.6 Embargo Pitch

```
SUBJECT: Embargoed: [brief description of news] -- [embargo date]

Hi [First Name],

We have news we'd like to share under embargo ahead of our [date] announcement.
Before I send details, I want to confirm you're comfortable with an embargo
lifting on [date, time, timezone].

The short version: [1-2 sentences summarizing the news at a high level without
revealing confidential details].

If you're interested and can agree to the embargo terms, I'll send the full
press kit with [data / images / interview access].

Best,
[Name]
```

Important: Never send embargoed material until the journalist explicitly agrees
to the embargo terms. Get confirmation in writing (email reply is sufficient).
