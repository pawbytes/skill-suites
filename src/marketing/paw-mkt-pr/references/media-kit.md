# Media Kit and Press Page

> Contents, structure, and maintenance for media kits and press pages.

## Media Kit Contents

### Required

- **Company overview**: 2-3 paragraphs (what the company does, founding story, mission, key differentiator)
- **Key facts**: Founded, HQ location, team size, customer count, 3-5 key milestones
- **Leadership bios**: 2-3 sentences each, written in third person, with credentials and areas of expertise
- **Leadership headshots**: Professional, high-resolution (300 DPI minimum), downloadable in multiple sizes
- **Brand assets**: Logo pack (PNG + SVG, color + monochrome + reversed), brand colors (hex + RGB), approved fonts, usage guidelines
- **Media contact**: Name, email, phone, response time commitment

### Recommended

- **Recent press coverage**: Table with date, outlet, headline, link (5-10 most notable pieces)
- **Awards and recognition**: List with year and granting organization
- **Product screenshots / images**: High-resolution, with captions
- **Fact sheet / one-pager**: PDF with key company information at a glance
- **Spokesperson availability**: Who can speak on which topics, timezone, response time

## Press Page

Host at `/press` or `/newsroom`:

- All media kit contents above, downloadable
- Press releases in reverse chronological order
- "In the News" section with recent coverage links

**Update monthly** -- an outdated press page signals an inactive brand.
