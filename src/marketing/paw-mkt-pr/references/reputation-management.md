# Brand Reputation Management

> Online reputation monitoring, review response strategy, and thought leadership positioning.

## Online Reputation Monitoring

**Monitor**:
- Brand name (exact + misspellings)
- Key personnel names
- Product names
- Competitor mentions (share of voice)
- Industry keywords

**Tools**:
- Google Alerts (free minimum)
- Mention, Brand24, Brandwatch, Meltwater

## Review Response Strategy

| Review Type | Response Approach | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Positive (4-5 stars) | Thank by name, reference specific detail, invite back | 24-48 hours |
| Neutral (3 stars) | Thank, acknowledge concern, offer to improve | 24 hours |
| Negative (1-2 stars) | Apologize, take responsibility, offer resolution, move to DM | 12-24 hours |
| Fake/spam | Flag for removal, respond factually if public | Same day |

**Principles**:
- Respond to every review -- silence signals indifference
- Avoid arguing publicly (it escalates and audiences side with the reviewer)
- Offer specific solutions and follow up to confirm resolution

## Thought Leadership Positioning

Build founder or key executives as industry authorities:

**LinkedIn**: 3-5 posts/week, 1-2 articles/month -- bridge to paw-mkt-social

**Speaking**: Panels, events, webinars -- start local, build to national

**Bylines**: Opinion pieces in industry publications

**Podcast guesting**: 1-2/month -- see `./references/journalist-outreach.md` for pitch strategy

**Awards**: Industry awards and "best of" lists -- credibility plus backlinks

**Original research**: Publish data under the expert's name -- see `./references/digital-pr-campaigns.md`

## Share of Voice Tracking

Monitor brand mentions relative to competitors:
- Track monthly mention volume
- Track sentiment ratio (positive/negative/neutral)
- Track outlet tier distribution
- Track social engagement on brand mentions

Target: Parity or better than nearest competitor. Top performers achieve 1.5-3x share of voice vs leaders.
