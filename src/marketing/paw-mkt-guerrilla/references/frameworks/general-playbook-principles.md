# General Playbook Principles

### Before Every Playbook

1. Read brand context and SOSTAC plan. Ground tactics in strategy.
2. Check risk tolerance. Conservative brands use conservative plays.
3. Set clear success metrics before starting.
4. Assign a single owner for execution.
5. Define a kill criteria: at what point do you stop if results are not materializing?

### After Every Playbook

1. Document results in `./.pawbytes/marketing-suites/brands/{brand-slug}/operations/guerrilla/performance/` (or `campaigns/{type}-{slug}/guerrilla/performance/` if campaign-tied).
2. Calculate ROI: (revenue or value generated - cost) / cost.
3. Identify what to repeat, what to iterate, and what to abandon.
4. Feed learnings into the growth experiment backlog.
5. Share learnings with the team to build institutional knowledge.
