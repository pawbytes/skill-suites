# Product Hunt Launch (30-Day Prep)

### Goal
Launch on Product Hunt, achieve Top 5 Product of the Day, and convert traffic into signups/revenue.

### Prerequisites
- Working product (or convincing demo/beta).
- Landing page with clear value proposition and signup/trial flow.
- Analytics and conversion tracking in place.
- Email list or supporter network of at least 100 people (300-500 preferred).

### Phase 1: Preparation (Day -30 to Day -14)

**Day -30 to -28: Positioning**
1. Write your one-sentence value proposition. Test it on 10 people outside your team. If they cannot repeat it back, simplify.
2. Define your Product Hunt tagline (max 60 characters). Format: "[Verb] [benefit] [for whom]".
3. Identify your primary category and topics on Product Hunt.
4. Research the top 20 launches in your category from the last 6 months. Note their taglines, descriptions, upvote counts, and comment patterns.

**Day -27 to -21: Asset Creation**
1. Create the Product Hunt gallery images (1270x760px recommended):
   - Image 1: Hero shot showing the product in action.
   - Image 2: Key features overview.
   - Image 3: Before/after or problem/solution.
   - Image 4: Social proof (logos, metrics, testimonials).
   - Image 5: Pricing or call to action.
2. Record a demo video (90-120 seconds):
   - First 10 seconds: the problem.
   - Next 60 seconds: the solution in action.
   - Final 20 seconds: results and call to action.
3. Write the Product Hunt description:
   - Paragraph 1: What it is and who it is for.
   - Paragraph 2: Key features and differentiators.
   - Paragraph 3: Why now / what is new.
   - Include a "Maker's comment" draft with your personal story.

**Day -21 to -14: Community Building**
1. Engage on Product Hunt daily: upvote, comment, and support other launches.
2. Connect with 10-20 active Product Hunt community members. Genuine engagement, not transactional.
3. Identify and reach out to 3-5 potential "hunters" (people with large followings on PH).
4. Begin teasing the launch on social media (X, LinkedIn): share behind-the-scenes content.
5. Start a "launch supporters" list via email or community (Slack, Discord).

### Phase 2: Pre-Launch (Day -14 to Day -1)

**Day -14 to -7:**
1. Finalize all Product Hunt page assets.
2. Send preview link to 10-20 trusted supporters for feedback.
3. Load-test your product and landing page. Launch day traffic can spike 10-100x.
4. Prepare response templates for common Product Hunt comments:
   - "What makes this different from [competitor]?"
   - "How does pricing work?"
   - "What is the roadmap?"
   - "Can it do [specific feature]?"
5. Set up a dedicated Slack/Discord channel for launch day coordination.

**Day -7 to -3:**
1. Send "launch is coming" email to your supporter list with the date and time.
2. Brief supporters on engagement guidelines:
   - "Engage authentically -- upvote if you genuinely like it, leave a real comment."
   - "Product Hunt detects and penalizes coordinated/fake engagement."
   - "Do NOT directly ask for upvotes. Share the page and let people decide."
3. Schedule social media posts for launch day (X, LinkedIn, Instagram, communities).

**Day -1:**
1. Final check: all assets uploaded, page reviewed, product stable.
2. Send "launching tomorrow" reminder to supporters with exact time.
3. Get sleep. Launch day is a marathon.

### Phase 3: Launch Day (Day 0)

**Timing**: Launch at 00:01 AM Pacific Time. The PH day resets at midnight PT.

**Hour 0-2 (Midnight - 2 AM PT):**
1. Publish the Product Hunt page.
2. Post your Maker's Comment immediately -- personal, authentic, tell the story.
3. Notify inner circle supporters via direct message/email.
4. Post on X/Twitter and LinkedIn with native content (no link in main post on LinkedIn; add link in first comment).

**Hour 2-6:**
1. Respond to every comment on Product Hunt within 30 minutes. Detailed, helpful responses.
2. Monitor social media mentions and engage.
3. Share progress updates with supporters ("We're #3 right now!").
4. Post in relevant communities (Slack, Discord, Reddit) where you have established presence.

**Hour 6-12:**
1. Continue responding to all comments.
2. Share a "mid-day update" on social media with a key insight or user story.
3. Engage with other launches happening the same day (goodwill + visibility).

**Hour 12-24:**
1. Maintain engagement on all comments.
2. Post a "final hours" update on social media.
3. Thank early supporters publicly.
4. Start collecting email addresses from PH visitors who hit your landing page.

### Phase 4: Post-Launch (Day +1 to +30)

1. **Day +1**: Publish a launch retrospective on your blog/social: what happened, metrics, lessons.
2. **Day +1-7**: Follow up with every person who commented on Product Hunt. Offer personal onboarding, a demo, or exclusive access.
3. **Day +3-7**: Repurpose launch content: LinkedIn post about the experience, X thread with lessons, blog post with data.
4. **Day +7-14**: Analyze conversion data. What percentage of PH visitors signed up? What was the cost per acquisition (if any paid promotion)?
5. **Day +14-30**: Convert launch momentum into sustained growth. Feature the Product Hunt badge on your site. Continue engaging with the PH community.

### Measurement
- Upvotes and ranking.
- Website traffic from Product Hunt (track via UTM: `?ref=producthunt`).
- Signups/trials from PH traffic.
- Revenue attributed to launch.
- Email subscribers captured.
- Social media follower growth.
- Backlinks earned (Product Hunt page itself is a high-DA backlink).

### Common Pitfalls
- Asking for upvotes directly (PH penalizes this).
- Launching on Monday or Friday (lower traffic).
- Not responding to comments (kills momentum).
- Treating PH as a one-day event instead of a campaign.
- No conversion funnel -- traffic arrives but has nowhere to go.
- Product instability on launch day.
