# Zero-Budget Launch (Waitlist + Beta + Referral)

### Goal
Launch a product with zero marketing budget by leveraging waitlists, beta programs, and referral mechanics.

### Phase 1: Waitlist (Pre-Product)

1. Create a landing page with:
   - Compelling headline describing the problem you solve.
   - Brief description of the solution (4-6 sentences).
   - Email capture form: "Join the waitlist for early access."
   - Social proof (if available): "Join 500+ others waiting."
2. Add a referral mechanic:
   - After signup: "Share your unique link to move up the list."
   - Show their position: "You are #247 in line."
   - Reward: "Refer 3 friends to get priority access."
3. Promote the waitlist page via:
   - Personal social media posts (X, LinkedIn).
   - Relevant communities where you have credibility.
   - Direct outreach to 50-100 target users.
   - Build-in-public updates about the product you are building.
4. Send weekly updates to the waitlist: development progress, sneak peeks, and surveys.

### Phase 2: Beta Program (Pre-Launch)

1. Invite the top 50-100 waitlist members (highest referrals, most engaged).
2. Create a private community (Slack, Discord) for beta testers.
3. Give beta testers a mission: "Help us make this great before we launch publicly."
4. Collect feedback aggressively. Implement visible changes based on feedback.
5. Ask beta testers to write reviews, testimonials, and case studies.
6. Ask: "Would you be willing to share this with others who might benefit?"

### Phase 3: Public Launch

1. Use the Product Hunt Playbook (Playbook 1) for launch day.
2. Invite the full waitlist with exclusive early-bird pricing or features.
3. Activate beta testers as launch advocates -- they have social proof and ownership.
4. Leverage all testimonials, reviews, and case studies collected during beta.
5. Implement a referral program from day one (see below).

### Referral Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| Double-sided incentive | Both referrer and referred get a benefit (credit, extended trial, free month). |
| Milestone rewards | Refer 1 friend = X. Refer 5 = Y. Refer 10 = Z. Escalating rewards drive volume. |
| Leaderboard | Public ranking of top referrers. Competition drives engagement. |
| Social sharing | Pre-written messages + one-click share to X, LinkedIn, email. |
| In-product prompts | Show referral CTA after moments of delight (completed task, achieved result). |
