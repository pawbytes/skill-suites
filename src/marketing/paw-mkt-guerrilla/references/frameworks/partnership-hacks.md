# Partnership Hacks (Co-Marketing, Cross-Promotion, Integration Directories)

### Goal
Leverage partner audiences and ecosystems to grow without paid acquisition.

### Tactic A: Newsletter Swaps

1. Identify 10-20 newsletters with audiences that overlap yours but are not competitive.
2. Pitch a swap: "I'll mention your newsletter to my [X] subscribers, you mention mine to yours."
3. Write a dedicated paragraph (not just a one-line mention) recommending the partner newsletter.
4. Track signups via unique UTM links.
5. Start with smaller newsletters (1K-10K subscribers) to build a track record, then approach larger ones.

### Tactic B: Co-Created Content

1. Partner with a complementary brand to co-create a resource:
   - Joint webinar (each brand brings their audience).
   - Co-authored ebook or guide (split the writing and the leads).
   - Joint research report (each brand contributes data).
   - Co-branded template or tool (each brand adds their expertise).
2. Both brands promote through their channels.
3. Gate the resource with a shared lead capture form (leads go to both partners).
4. Follow up with leads independently.

### Tactic C: Integration Directories

1. Build integrations with tools your audience already uses (Slack, Zapier, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.).
2. List your product in partner marketplaces and directories:
   - Zapier App Directory.
   - Slack App Directory.
   - HubSpot App Marketplace.
   - Salesforce AppExchange.
   - Chrome Web Store.
   - VS Code Marketplace (for developer tools).
3. Optimize your listing:
   - Keyword-rich title and description.
   - Screenshots and demo video.
   - Reviews and ratings (ask happy users to review).
4. Each listing is a permanent, free acquisition channel with high-intent traffic.

### Tactic D: Cross-Promotion Bundles

1. Partner with 3-5 complementary products to create a bundle:
   - "The [Category] Starter Pack: [Product A] + [Product B] + [Product C] for [discounted price]."
2. Each product promotes the bundle to their audience.
3. The combined audience reach is 3-5x any individual product.
4. AppSumo, StackSocial, and similar platforms facilitate bundle deals.
5. Use bundles for launch events, holiday promotions, or industry conferences.

### Tactic E: Affiliate Micro-Networks

1. Recruit 10-20 micro-influencers or niche bloggers with small but engaged audiences (1K-10K followers).
2. Offer generous commissions: 20-40% recurring for SaaS, $50-$200 per referral for services.
3. Provide each affiliate with:
   - Unique tracking links.
   - Pre-written content (social posts, email copy, review templates).
   - Product access and training so they can speak authentically.
4. 20 affiliates x 2,000 followers each = 40,000 reach for zero upfront cost.
5. Pay only for results: no risk, pure performance.

### Measurement for All Partnership Tactics

| Metric | Tracking Method |
|---|---|
| Referral traffic | UTM parameters per partner |
| Signups from partners | Unique landing pages or promo codes per partner |
| Revenue attributed | Referral/affiliate tracking links |
| Cost per acquisition | Commission/cost / signups |
| Partner satisfaction | Quarterly check-in and feedback |
| Pipeline from partnerships | CRM attribution |
