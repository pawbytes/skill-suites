# Part 2: Quickstart Guide

## Your First Social Post in 10 Minutes

This guide walks you through creating your first social media post from start to finish. By the end, you'll have a professional image ready to upload to Instagram.

**Time required:** Approximately 10 minutes

**What you'll need:**

- Your brand name
- A brief description of what you want (1-2 sentences)
- Optional: Brand colors, logo, or reference images you like

---

## The Scenario: Sarah's Bakery First Post

Meet Sarah. She owns a small bakery called "Morning Glow Bakery." She's never used the Creative Suite before. She wants to announce a new product on Instagram.

Here's exactly what happens, step by step.

---

## Step 1: Say Hello to Aria

**What You Do:**

Type a simple greeting to start.

**Example:**
```
You: "Hi Aria, I need help creating an Instagram post"
```

**What Happens:**

Aria responds with a warm greeting and asks what you need.

**Aria's Response:**
```
Aria: "Hi! I'd love to help you create an Instagram post. 
       What would you like the post to be about?"
```

**Time: 30 seconds**

---

## Step 2: Describe What You Want

**What You Do:**

Describe your post in plain language. Include what you're promoting and any key details.

**Example:**
```
You: "I run a bakery called Morning Glow Bakery. We just launched
     a new cinnamon roll and I want to announce it on Instagram.
     The rolls are huge and gooey with cream cheese frosting."
```

**What Happens:**

Aria checks if your brand exists in memory. If it's your first time, she offers to set up your brand.

**Aria's Response:**
```
Aria: "Morning Glow Bakery sounds delicious! I don't have your 
       brand set up yet. Would you like to do a quick brand setup 
       so all your posts are consistent? It takes about 2 minutes."
```

**Time: 1 minute**

---

## Step 3: Set Up Your Brand (First Time Only)

**What You Do:**

Answer Aria's questions about your brand.

**Aria's Questions:**
```
Aria: "Let's set up Morning Glow Bakery. A few quick questions:
       
       1. Do you have brand colors? (If not, I can suggest some)
       2. What feeling should your brand convey? 
          (cozy, modern, playful, elegant, etc.)
       3. Do you have a logo file you can share?
       4. Who is your target customer?"
```

**Your Answers:**
```
You: "1. I like warm browns and creamy colors - like coffee and milk
     2. Cozy and welcoming, like a neighborhood bakery
     3. Yes, I have a logo. Let me share the file.
     4. Local families and coffee lovers"
```

**What Happens:**

Aria saves your brand information. The Designer will use this for every future post.

**Aria's Response:**
```
Aria: "Perfect! I've saved your brand profile:
       
       Brand: Morning Glow Bakery
       Colors: Warm brown (#5D4037), Cream (#FFF8E1)
       Style: Cozy, welcoming, neighborhood feel
       Audience: Local families and coffee lovers
       
       Now, let's create that Instagram post! I'll connect you with 
       our Designer who specializes in social media visuals."
```

**Time: 2 minutes**

---

## Step 4: The Designer Takes Over

**What You Do:**

The Designer activates and asks for specifics about your post.

**Designer's Response:**
```
Designer: "Hi Sarah! I'll create your cinnamon roll announcement post.
           A few quick questions:
           
           1. What text should appear on the post? (headline, any details)
           2. Do you want the price shown?
           3. Any specific style preference? (photo-heavy, illustration, 
              minimal, bold typography)"
```

**Your Answers:**
```
You: "1. Headline: 'Introducing Our New Giant Cinnamon Rolls'
        Subtext: 'Warm, gooey, and covered in cream cheese frosting'
     2. Yes, show $4.99
     3. I want it to look appetizing - maybe a photo style?"
```

**What Happens:**

The Designer creates your post using your brand colors and the details you provided.

**Designer's Response:**
```
Designer: "Got it! Creating your Instagram post now:
           
           - Dimensions: 1080 x 1350 pixels (4:5 ratio for maximum 
             Instagram presence)
           - Style: Warm, appetizing photo-style with your brand colors
           - Text: Headline + subtext + price
           
           Generating now... this takes about 30 seconds."
```

**Time: 2 minutes**

---

## Step 5: Review Your Post

**What You Do:**

The Designer shows you the created post. Review it and decide if you want changes.

**Designer Shows:**
```
Designer: "Here's your Instagram post!"
          
          [Image appears: A warm-toned post showing a delicious 
           cinnamon roll with the headline "Introducing Our New 
           Giant Cinnamon Rolls" in an appetizing font, subtext 
           below, and $4.99 in a badge]
          
          What do you think? Would you like any changes?
```

**Example Reactions:**

If you love it:
```
You: "It looks perfect!"
```

If you want changes:
```
You: "Can you make the headline bigger? And can we change 
     'Giant' to 'Mega'?"
```

**Designer's Response to Changes:**
```
Designer: "Absolutely! Updating now..."
          
          [Updated image appears with larger headline and 'Mega 
           Cinnamon Rolls']
          
          How's this?
```

**Time: 2 minutes**

---

## Step 6: Export Your Post

**What You Do:**

Confirm you're happy and ask for the file.

**Example:**
```
You: "Looks great! Can I get the file?"
```

**What Happens:**

The Designer saves the file to your brand folder.

**Designer's Response:**
```
Designer: "Your post is ready! I've saved it to:
           
           brands/morning-glow-bakery/social/
               instagram-cinnamon-roll-launch.png
           
           This file is:
           - Ready to upload to Instagram
           - Correct dimensions (1080 x 1350)
           - Optimized file size
           
           Just upload it directly to Instagram - no editing needed!"
```

**Time: 30 seconds**

---

## Step 7: Done! What You Received

**Your Output:**

```
brands/morning-glow-bakery/
    guidelines.md                    <-- Your brand info (saved)
    assets/
        logo.png                     <-- Your logo (saved)
    social/
        instagram-cinnamon-roll-launch.png   <-- Ready to post!
```

**File Details:**

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Filename | `instagram-cinnamon-roll-launch.png` |
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1350 pixels |
| Format | PNG (high quality) |
| Ready for | Instagram Feed |
| Upload method | Direct upload, no editing needed |

## Common Questions

### What if I don't like the first result?

Just say what you want changed:
- "Make the text bigger"
- "Can we try a different color?"
- "I want it more minimal"
- "Can you add my logo?"

The Designer will revise until you're happy.

### What if I don't have a brand yet?

Aria can help you create one. When she asks about brand colors, you can say:
- "I don't have colors yet, what do you suggest?"
- "I like the style of [shows a reference image]"
- "Pick something that fits a [cozy/modern/playful] bakery"

### What platforms can I create for?

| Platform | Dimensions the Designer Uses |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Instagram Feed | 1080 x 1350 pixels (4:5 ratio) |
| Instagram Story | 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio) |
| Instagram Reel Cover | 1080 x 1920 pixels |
| LinkedIn Post | 1200 x 627 pixels |
| Twitter/X | 1600 x 900 pixels |
| Facebook Post | 1200 x 630 pixels |
| TikTok | 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio) |

Just specify the platform when you make your request.

### What if I need multiple posts?

Just ask:
```
You: "I need 5 Instagram posts for our summer menu launch"
```

The Designer will create all 5 with consistent branding.
