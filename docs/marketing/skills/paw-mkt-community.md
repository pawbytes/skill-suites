# paw-mkt-community

## Overview

Builds community-led growth systems and engagement programs.

## When to use it

Use `/paw-mkt-community` when you need:
- a community strategy
- a 90-day community launch plan
- engagement programming and content calendar
- moderation structure
- gamification or ambassador program design
- member onboarding flow
- scaling or platform migration guidance
- crisis management playbooks

This skill focuses on owned community platforms (Discord, Slack, Circle, Skool, etc.) — not general social media management (use `paw-mkt-social`).

## Inputs to prepare

- community platform or plan
- audience segments
- participation goals
- moderation capacity
- current member behavior if known

## Deliverables

- community strategy docs
- 90-day launch plan
- programming and content calendar ideas
- onboarding flow design
- gamification structure
- ambassador program
- operating playbooks
- engagement campaigns
- scaling playbook
- crisis management guide

## Framework files

12 individual framework files, indexed in `frameworks-index.csv`:
