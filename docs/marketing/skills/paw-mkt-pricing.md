# `marketing-pricing`

## Overview

Helps with pricing strategy, packaging, value metrics, and pricing-page decisions.

## When to use it

Use `/paw-mkt-pricing` when you need:
- pricing strategy direction
- tier design
- value metric decisions
- pricing page messaging
- price increase planning

## Inputs to prepare

- current pricing
- customer segments
- competitor context
- margin or packaging constraints
- pricing goals

## Deliverables

- pricing strategy docs
- pricing research plans
- competitive analysis
- pricing-page recommendations

## Output locations

```text
brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/pricing/
```

## Related skills

- `marketing-sales`
- `marketing-cro`
- `marketing-sostac`

## Sample prompts

```text
/paw-mkt-pricing
Help us redesign our pricing tiers.
```

```text
/paw-mkt-pricing
For Acorn Legal, compare our current pricing against likely competitors and recommend a stronger packaging model.
```

```text
/paw-mkt-pricing
Use our positioning and target segments to propose a value metric and pricing-page narrative.
```
