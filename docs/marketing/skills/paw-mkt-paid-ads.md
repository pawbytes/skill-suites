# paw-mkt-paid-ads

## Overview

Builds paid acquisition strategy, creative, campaign structure, testing plans, and performance iteration.

## When to use it

Use `/paw-mkt-paid-ads` when you need:
- campaign planning
- ad copy and creative angles
- audience targeting ideas
- budget allocation
- testing plans

## Inputs to prepare

- target channels
- budget
- audience and offer
- landing page context
- conversion goal

## Deliverables

- campaign briefs
- ad copy sets
- audience specs
- budget plans
- A/B test plans

## Output locations

```text
.pawbytes/marketing-suites/brands/{brand-slug}/campaigns/paid-ads/
```

## Related skills

- `paw-mkt-cro`
- `paw-mkt-analytics`
- `paw-mkt-launch`
- `paw-mkt-video`
- `paw-mkt-psychology`

## Sample prompts

```text
/paw-mkt-paid-ads
Build a paid acquisition plan for our next quarter.
```

```text
/paw-mkt-paid-ads
For Acorn Legal, create a Meta and Google Ads campaign brief to drive demo requests.
```

```text
/paw-mkt-paid-ads
Use our SOSTAC tactics and product marketing context to draft three ad angles plus matching audience specs.
```
